## Overview

King's Quest VII features a unique chapter-based structure where you can choose which chapter to start with, though playing in order (Chapters 1-6) is highly recommended for story coherence. The game uses a simple point-and-click interface - if your cursor blinks or sparkles over something, you can interact with it.

### Game Features

- **Bookmarking System**: Instead of traditional save games (Version 1.0 has 1 bookmark, Version 2.0 has multiple)
- **Dual Protagonists**: Play as both Queen Valanice and Princess Rosella
- **Chapter Selection**: Non-linear chapter progression available
- **No Death Gameplay**: Unlike earlier King's Quest games, death is extremely rare
- **Interface**: Single cursor that sparkles when interactive elements are available

### Inventory Tips

üí° **Always examine items closely**: Click items on the Egyptian eye in your toolbar for close-up views. You can rotate items by dragging left/right - many have hidden surprises!

## Chapter 1: "Where In Blazes Am I?"

**Character**: Valanice | **Location**: Desert

### Opening Desert Sequence

- Watch the opening sand cyclone sequence
- **Take** the **petticoat piece** that gets torn off Valanice
- Observe the kangaroo rat being chased by a jackalope

### Cave Exploration

- Go **north** to the cave opening
- Examine the **drawings** on the right outside wall (click twice for close-up)
  - üìù **Important**: Make note of these symbols for later reference
- Enter the cave and **pick up the basket**
- Examine the four clay pots:
  - Click on any **three pots** (they'll break and annoy Valanice)
  - **Take** the **fourth clay pot**

### Inventory Examination

üí° **Critical Inventory Tip**: Always examine items closely by clicking them on the Egyptian eye icon. You can rotate items by dragging - this reveals hidden items!

#### Examining the Basket

- **Open** the basket lid
- **Rotate** the basket until you see a small yellow dot
- **Click** on the yellow dot to take the **corn kernel**

#### Other Important Items

- **Examine** the ornate **comb** (Valanice's memento of Rosella)
- **Use** the comb on Valanice to make her cry (important for later!)

### Growing the Corn

- Go **west** of the cave to find a **damp spot** in the sand
- **Plant** the **corn kernel** in the damp sand
- Watch it magically grow into a huge corn stalk
- **Take** the **corn cob**

### The Portal Door

- Continue **west** to the **colossus head** (large portal door)
- **Examine** the **arrow design** above the door (close-up view)
  - üìù **Note**: Something arrow-shaped fits in this hole - remember this!

### Kangaroo Rat's Problem

- Go **east** past the cave to the **"Rare Curiosities"** door
- **Knock** on the door to meet the kangaroo rat
- Learn about his problem: the jackalope stole his **glasses**
  - üéØ **Goal**: Retrieve the glasses to gain the rat's help

### Temple and Statue Pool Area

- Go **south** to the large temple (don't enter yet)
- Go **west** to return to the first screen, then continue **west**
- Find a **little pool** with a large kneeling statue

#### Items to Collect

- **Take** the **stick** lying on the ground
- **Look** inside the pool (something interesting is down there!)
- **Take** the **salt crystals** from the southern rim of the pool
- **Fill** the clay pot with salt water from the pool
- **Taste** the water (it's salty!)

### Water Purification Ritual

At the base of the statue are drawings showing how to create fresh water:

1. **Pour** salt water into the statue's bowl
2. **Use** the comb on the bowl (makes Valanice cry into it)
3. **Place** the corn cob in the statue's other hand
4. The statue performs magic to purify the water
5. **Take** the fresh water (save it - don't drink!)

‚ö†Ô∏è **Note**: Make sure the statue head shows the moon-water god, not the sun god. Click the head to change if needed.

### Finding the Desert Spirit

- Head **south** into the endless desert (stay close to familiar areas!)
- Wander 1-3 screens around the regular desert areas
- Find the **desert spirit** and talk to him quickly
- **Give** him the fresh water to free him from his thirst
- Choose either the **rope** or **bug reducing powder** from his bag
  - üóíÔ∏è **Rope**: For trapping the jackalope
  - üóíÔ∏è **Powder**: For eliminating scorpions completely

#### If You Chose the Horn (with powder)

- If you selected powder, look around for a **horn** in the sand
- **Take** the horn if found

### Catching the Jackalope

#### Method A: Using the Rope

- **Tie** the rope between the two large green cactus plants on the path
- Wait for the jackalope to run down the path and get tripped
- **Take** the **spectacles** and **jackalope fur**

#### Method B: Using the Horn

- **Blow** the horn to clear sand from it
- **Use** the horn on ground holes where the jackalope pops out
- **Take** the **spectacles** and **jackalope fur** when he's stunned

### Trading with the Kangaroo Rat

- Return the **spectacles** to the kangaroo rat
- Try showing him various items to see what he'll trade
- Remember: he speaks in rhymes and offers rhyming items!

### Temple Puzzle

#### Preparing for the Scorpion

- **If using rope**: Combine **petticoat piece** + **stick** = flag
- **If using powder**: Have the powder ready

#### Inside the Temple

- Enter the temple and face the huge scorpion
- **Use flag** (traps it temporarily) OR **use powder** (eliminates it permanently)

#### The Altar Puzzle

- Examine the altar table on the right
- **Turn** the upside-down teardrop symbol right-side up
- Place **yellow** and **blue** gems in the statue's hands
- Place the **pink-red** gem on the sun symbol
- **Take** the **arrow-shaped puzzle piece**

### Completing the Portal

- **Take** the **seed** from the colorful gourd near the cave
- **Trade** the seed to the kangaroo rat for a **blue bead**

#### Draining the Pool

- Change statue head to **sun god** (rays coming out)
- Follow the cave drawings to position the neck beads correctly
- **Turn** the bowl upside down
- The pool drains, revealing stairs

#### Getting the Second Puzzle Piece

- Go down into the drained pool
- **Put** the blue bead in the offering bowl
- **Take** the correct V-shaped puzzle piece (matches your arrow piece)

#### Opening the Portal

- **Combine** both puzzle pieces in your inventory
- **Use** the combined piece on the hole above the colossus door
- Before entering, **use stick** on cactus next to door
- **Take** the **prickly pear fruit**
- Enter the portal to proceed to Chapter 2

## Chapter 2: "A Troll Is As A Troll Does"

**Character**: Rosella | **Location**: Vulcanix Underground

### The Transformation

- Watch Rosella's transformation into a troll
- Note the Troll King's **green eyes** in person vs. **purple eyes** in portrait
- **Look** in the mirror, then **examine** the king's portrait
- **Open** the door and leave the bedroom

### Meeting Mathilde

- In the Great Hall, **take** the **mechanical rat** dropped by the bratty troll
- **Talk** to Mathilde (old lady troll) to learn what you need:
  - Gold bowl
  - Emerald water
  - Dragon scale
  - Silver spoon
  - Baked beetles

### The Kitchen

- Go **east** to the kitchen
- **Wind up** the mechanical rat and set it loose
- When the cook leaves to chase it:
  - **Take** **baked beetles** from the bin
  - **Take** a **gold bowl** from the cupboard
  - **Examine** the bowl - make sure it says "14k gold" not "brass"

### The Mudbaths Area

- Go **northwest** to the mudbaths
- Listen to the **female trolls** discuss:
  - How to make trolls fall asleep (sulfur)
  - How to wake them up (loud noises)

### The Metalsmith Area

- Go **west** to the metalsmithing area
- **Talk** to the obnoxious metalsmith
- **Talk** to the nice jeweler (learns of his dream to leave)
- Enter the **cave** in the back of the screen

#### In the Cave

- **Use** gold bowl on rock formations to get **emerald water**
- **Take** the **lantern** from the back wall
- Cross the **windy chasm** to reach the sulfur
  - Jump to middle stone, then to ledge (may take multiple tries)
  - **Take** the **sulfur**

### Dealing with the Metalsmith

- **Use** wet sulfur on fire near the metalsmith (knocks him out)
- **Take** **tongs** from tool rack
- **Use** tongs on the hot **mold box**
- **Cool** the box in the water bucket
- **Take** the **silver spoon**
- **Light** the lantern at the forge (use bellows first)

### Getting the Dragon Scale

- Go to the throne area and **take** the **round shield**
- **Remove** the center spike from the shield
- Go **southwest** to the broken wagon and bridge

#### The Bridge Troll Trick

- **Examine** the broken wagon closely (close-up view)
- **Put** the shield (minus spike) on the wheel spoke
- **Reattach** the center spike
- **Click** on wagon to ride it and knock off the bridge troll!

#### The Crystal Dragon

- Cross the bridge and zigzag path to reach the crystal dragon
- **Talk** to the sad dragon
- **Give** the lantern to the dragon (it needs the spark)
- Dragon flies away and gives you a **large gem**

### Completing the Transformation Items

- **Give** the large gem to the jeweler
- **Get** his hammer and chisel
- **Return** to the sleeping crystal dragon
- Wait for tail to drop, then quickly **use hammer and chisel** on tail
- **Take** the **dragon scale**

### The Transformation

- Return to Mathilde in the Great Hall
- **Give** her all five items starting with the gold bowl:
  1. Gold bowl
  2. Emerald water
  3. Dragon scale
  4. Silver spoon
  5. Baked beetles
- Watch the transformation back to human form!

### Escaping the Bedroom

- **Look** in mirror to see human Rosella again
- Stack furniture under the smoking portrait:
  - Largest piece first
  - Medium piece on top
  - Smallest piece last
- **Climb** the stack and **click** on portrait to escape

### The Real Troll King Mystery

- **Take** the **dragon toad** that fell when you slid down
- **Talk** to Mathilde about the toad
- Learn the real Troll King is in Ooga Booga
- **Get** the **magic rope** from Mathilde

### Final Escape

- When Malicia confronts you, **use mechanical rat** on her (she hates rats!)
- Go to the bucket elevator and **use magic rope** to fix it
- **Get** in bucket and **pull** rope to escape to Chapter 3

## Chapter 3: "The Sky Is Falling!"

**Character**: Valanice | **Location**: Woods and Falderal

### Dealing with the Gila Monster

- Face the Gila monster from Chapter 1's ending
- **Use** **prickly pear fruit** to feed it (it loves these!)
- Continue north when it leaves

### The Woods

- Arrive at beautiful woods with two white statue maidens
- Note the dried riverbed and broken bridge
- Go **west** to find **Attis the Stag** by the large oak tree

#### Meeting Attis

- **Talk** to Attis repeatedly until he has nothing new to say
- Learn about his curse (he's a tree, Ceres is a stag)
- **Use** Rosella's **comb** on the stag to learn about the werewoods

### Forest Exploration

- Go **west** one more screen (but don't enter werewoods yet)
- Go **north** to riverbed with stepping stones
- Carefully **cross** the stones (watch Valanice's balance!)

#### Saving the Hummingbird

- **Trap** the spider in your **basket**
- **Free** the hummingbird from the web

### Entering Falderal

- Continue north to Falderal's gate
- The gatekeeper has ridiculous demands (ignore them)
- **Click** on small door on right to enter

#### Meeting Archduke Fifi

- **Use** the **comb** on the hyperactive poodle (Archduke Fifi)
- Listen to Valanice's tragic story
- üí° **Important**: He says "Take the Faux Shop with a grain of salt"

### Exploring Falderal

#### The China Shop

- Enter and **talk** to Fernando the Bull about his stolen **Treasure** (china bird)
- Notice the lovely **pink mask** on the wall

#### The Pool Area

- **Talk** to the mockingbird in the tree (for insults!)
- Note the snake oil salesman's wagon

#### Finding Treasure

- **Uncover** the cage next to the snake oil wagon
- **Talk** to **Treasure** the china bird
- **Take** Treasure and **return** her to Fernando
- **Get** the **pink mask** as reward

### The Masquerade Ball

- **Put on** the pink mask
- **Enter** the Town Hall
- Enjoy the Archduke's birthday celebration
- **Taste** the cake if desired
- Go to back of room and **click** on tapestry

#### Navigating the Crazy Stairs

- In the upside-down stairway room, go **right**
- Follow stairs to reach the **powder room** (rightside up doorway)
- **Click** door twice to enter

#### Getting the Magic Statuette

- **Use** third mirror from right (upside-down Valanice) to reach office
- **Open** desk drawer and **take** **magic statuette**
- **Use** comb on statuette to see vision of Rosella

### The Falling Moon

- **Exit** Town Hall - the "moon" falls into the pool!
- **Talk** to the chicken (she was right about the sky falling!)
- **Take** **wooden nickel** from mockingbird's nest

### The Faux Shop

- **Eat** salt crystals in front of the Faux Shop
- **Enter** the shop (salt makes you enter instead of walk through)
- **Talk** to the turtle owner
- **Buy** a book with the wooden nickel
- **Trade** the pink mask for the **rubber chicken**
- **Remove** the tail feather from rubber chicken

### Forest Solutions

#### Finding the Rock Spirit

- Leave Falderal and go **east** from the spider web screen
- Find the sleeping **Rock Spirit**
- **Use** rubber chicken feather to **tickle** his nose
- **Talk** to him to learn about sacred nectar and food needed

#### Starting the River of Life

- Return to white statue maidens screen
- Go to upper left area to find **hummingbirds**
- **Talk** to the hummingbird you saved
- **Use** clay pot on flowers to catch **nectar**
- **Use** pot on statue maiden's **pitcher**
- Watch river start flowing and bridge get fixed!

### Completing the Chapter

- **Trade** the book to kangaroo rat for a **long crook**
- Return to Falderal and **use crook** on "moon" in pool to fish it out
- Watch Valanice get arrested as Chapter 3 ends

## Chapter 4: "Will The Real Troll King Please Stand Up?"

**Character**: Rosella | **Location**: Ooga Booga Land of the Dead

### The Elevator Collapse

- The bucket elevator is collapsing - act quickly!
- **Take** the **shovel** from the gravedigger
- **Talk** to gravedigger twice to learn about his machine and special rat

### Exploring Ooga Booga

- **Examine** graffiti on tomb doors (note the three images)
- Beware the tall boogeyman - **run** if you see him!
- Note the barking ghost dog and crying cloaked lady (ignore for now)

### The Ghoul Kids' Treehouse

- Go **south** to the pumpkin treehouse
- Check periodically throughout chapter for ghoul kid activities
- When bucket elevator is available, **enter** the treehouse quickly
- **Take** the **backbone** and **foot-in-a-bag**
- Return to ground level

### The Coroner's House

- Go **west** to Dr. Cadaver's house
- **Talk** to him to learn he needs a backbone
- **Give** him the backbone and watch him swallow it!
- **Get** the **weird pet in a box**

### Getting the Gravedigger's Rat

- Return to ghoul kids when they're tossing the rat around
- Listen to their chant about the boogeyman's S-shaped stick:
  - Stick up = boogeyman home (run and hide!)
  - Stick down = boogeyman away (safe to approach)
- **Trade** weird pet for the **rat** (use bucket elevator)
- **Return** rat to gravedigger
- **Get** the **horn** to summon him for digging

### Freeing the Cat

- When ghoul kids try to bury the live cat:
- **Use** hammer and chisel on coffin to free the **cat**
- Cat gives you an **extra life** and information about real Troll King

### Finding the Real Troll King

- Go to the **deadfall** (boogeyman's bone pile)
- Make sure the **S-shaped branch** is pointing **down**
- **Blow** the horn to summon gravedigger
- Tell him to **dig** on the bone pile
- **Go down** the hole he digs

#### The Combination Lock

- **Use** combination on coffin lock: **skull, bat, spider**
- Meet **King Otar** (the real Troll King)
- **Show** him his **dragon toad**
- **Use** hammer and chisel on his bracelet to get **jewel**
- Watch escape sequence - get black cloak, King becomes scarab

### Preparing to Leave Ooga Booga

- **Wear** the black cloak as disguise
- **Talk** to coroner to get **defoliant spray**
- **Take** shovel from gravedigger's site

### The Swamp Journey

- Go **south** to swamp
- **Use** defoliant spray on green swamp monster
- Go **east** to three-headed plant
- **Feed** foot-in-a-bag to plant while grabbing the **flower** at its base

### Infiltrating Malicia's House

- Continue **east** to Malicia's house
- Go to **back** of house (around right side)
- **Remove** roots and **use shovel** to enlarge hole
- Wait until dog stops barking before entering
- **Enter** via hole

#### Inside Malicia's House

- **Duck** under floorboards when Malicia enters
- **Use** defoliant spray on Cuddles' nose
- When they leave:
  - **Take** **mysterious device** from bedside dresser
  - **Take** **woolen stocking**
- **Exit** via floorboards, **wear** black cloak

### Defeating the Werebeast

- Go **east** to werewoods
- **Combine** silver spoon + woolen stocking = **sling**
- **Use** sling on werebeast's nose

### The Portal to Vulcanix Underground

- Continue **east** and **north** to Falderal
- Enter Town Hall via tapestry to crazy stairs room
- Go to **powder room**
- **Clean** cherub base with woolen stocking
- **Use** hammer and chisel on grape decoration
- **Put** grape in cherub's mouth
- **Use** wand on scarab to turn King Otar back to troll
- Watch him open the fountain portal
- **Enter** fountain and follow to mechanical room door
- Note the opening sequence: **left eye, right eye, nose**

## Chapter 5: "Nightmare In Etheria"

**Character**: Valanice | **Location**: Etheria and Beyond

### The Trial in Falderal

- Watch Valanice's ridiculous trial
- Learn she must get the "moon" back into the sky

### Getting the Werebeast Salve

- Go to snake oil salesman's wagon

- Go to Faux Shop and **buy rubber chicken** if you haven't already

- **Talk** to dog repeatedly

- Go to crypt with crying cloaked lady

#### Getting Firecracker and Skull

- Stand in middle of path and wait for Headless Horseman

### Exploring Etheria

- Go **east** and up winding mountain path
- Avoid the white flying horse (Sirocco) for now

#### Getting Ambrosia

- **Climb** twisted tree and **take** **ambrosia**
- Go back down mountain path

- Go **east** to find little flying dragonettes
- **Feed** them ambrosia and listen to their song

### Meeting the Fates

- Go **north** from dragonettes to gate-harp with gazing ball

### Finding a Place to Sleep

- **Use** fife to return to Ooga Booga

### Getting Help from Ceres

- **Play** fife to return to Etheria

- **Use** southwest rainbow to reach woods

- **Give** pomegranate to **Attis** to transform Ceres back to human
- **Talk** to Ceres about thawing Mab
- Learn you need a crystal shaft filled with sunlight

### Getting the Crystal Shaft

- **Use** fife to return to Etheria, then southwest rainbow to swamp
- Go to **back** of Malicia's house (wait until dog stops barking)
- **Enter** house and hide under floorboards
- **Give** ambrosia to Cuddles when it digs at hole
- When they leave, **take crystal shaft** from lamp

### Filling with Light

- **Use** northwest rainbow to reach desert
- Go to **temple** and **use crystal shaft** on light beam
- **Return** to Etheria via fife

### Accessing Dreamland While Awake

- **Show** crystal shaft to Fates (play song first)
- **Get** **dreamcatcher** from them
- Go up mountain to cave with nightmare monster
- **Use** dreamcatcher on nightmare to catch it
- **Enter** cave and **talk** to Dreamweaver
- **Show** dreamcatcher and ask about Dreamland
- **Get** **tapestry of dreams**
- **Use** tapestry on Valanice to reach Dreamland

#### In Dreamland

- **Use** dreamcatcher on Dreamland nightmare (they fight each other)
- While they fight, **swim** to shore of island
- **Enter** temple and **use crystal shaft** on Mab to thaw her
- **Get** **magic bridle** from Mab

### Catching Sirocco

- Go up mountain path to dreamweaver's cave
- Stand in **alcove** behind left edge of cave (partially hidden)
- When Sirocco flies by, **use magic bridle** on him
- May take several attempts to catch him successfully

### Chapter 5 Ending

- Watch as Valanice rides Sirocco
- See Borasco attack and strand Valanice on mountain
- Chapter 5 ends setting up the final confrontation

- Watch the two Troll Kings fighting

- **Use** flower on unconscious Troll King

- Watch reunion sequence until Malicia interrupts
- Malicia fights and kills Edgar
- **Take** mysterious device from wall

- **Use** device on Cuddles the dog for humorous transformation

- **Quickly use** black cat's **extra life** on Edgar

### Victory!

- Congratulations! You've completed King's Quest VII: The Princeless Bride!
- Watch the happy ending sequence

## Essential Tips and Strategies

### General Gameplay

- **Save frequently** using bookmarks (especially before difficult sequences)
- **Examine everything** - the cursor will sparkle on interactive items
- **Rotate inventory items** to find hidden objects and details
- **Talk to characters multiple times** - they often have more to say

### Chapter-Specific Notes

#### Chapter 1 (Valanice - Desert)

- **Make Valanice cry early** by using comb on her (needed for water purification)
- **Choose rope vs. powder** based on preference (both work for jackalope)
- **Don't venture too far** into endless desert (Valanice will die)

#### Chapter 2 (Rosella - Troll Land)

- **Check bowl marking** carefully (must be "14k gold" not "brass")
- **Time the crystal dragon's tail** carefully to avoid getting squished
- **Listen to mudbath trolls** for crucial sulfur information

#### Chapter 3 (Valanice - Woods/Falderal)

- **Balance carefully** on stepping stones across riverbed
- **Remember "grain of salt"** advice for entering Faux Shop
- **Act quickly** in powder room before getting trapped

#### Chapter 4 (Rosella - Ooga Booga)

- **Watch S-shaped branch** - only approach deadfall when it points down
- **Wait for dog to stop barking** before entering Malicia's house
- **Note door opening sequence** for later use in Chapter 6

#### Chapter 5 (Valanice - Etheria)

- **Start river of life** if not done in Chapter 3
- **Learn dragonettes' song** - first four notes are crucial

- Don't get in crystal dragon's tail path
- Don't linger when boogeyman's stick is up
