## Introduction

The Secret of Monkey Island is a comedic point-and-click adventure game filled with humor, memorable characters, and clever puzzles. This game pioneered many adventure gaming conventions and is notable for being virtually impossible to die in (unlike its Sierra counterparts).

### Important Notes

**No Dead Ends**: Unlike many adventure games of its era, you cannot get permanently stuck or die in The Secret of Monkey Island. Feel free to experiment!

**For Your Amusement**: This game is packed with humor and easter eggs. While this walkthrough focuses on completing the game, don't hesitate to explore dialogue options, examine everything, and try unusual combinations for entertainment value.

**Insult Sword Fighting**: One of the game's most memorable features involves learning and countering insults during sword duels. The responses are logical but require wit to figure out.

## Part I: The Three Trials

### Getting Started

- Walk down from the path to the docks
- Look at the poster of Governor Marley
- Go east across the docks to the **Scumm Bar** and open the door
- Walk inside and talk to various pirates:
  - Pirate in red coat and black hat (nearest the door)
  - Pirate sitting under the spinning anchor
  - Man at the round table (right center)
  - Dog next to the curtain (right side)
- Go east to the next room
- Talk to the **important-looking pirates** to learn about the three trials

### The Kitchen Scene

Wait for the cook to leave the kitchen (this may take a while). When he does:

- Continue waiting until he walks off screen to the left
- Go into the kitchen
- Get the **meat** and **pot** from under the table
- Open the back door and go onto the pier
- Look at the fish
- Walk onto the right end of the bottom board to tilt it like a seesaw
- Repeat until the seagull flies away
- Pick up the **herring**
- Leave the bar and watch the cutscene

### Initial Exploration

- Go west past the dock to the lookout point
- Walk through the archway to reach the island map
- Go to the "clearing" and walk into the circus tent
- Listen to the Fettucini Brothers' conversation
- Talk to them when given the chance
- Ask about payment (478 pieces of eight)
- Tell them you have a helmet
- Give them the **pot**
- Exit right when finished

### Gathering Supplies

**At the Village:**

- Go east through the archway to Low Street
- Talk to the **Citizen of MÃªlÃ©e** (man with parrot)
- Tell him you once had a barber called Dominique
- Buy the **map** from him
- Talk to the Men of Low Moral Fiber (optional)
- Enter the voodoo shop (center building)
- Look at everything and get the **chicken** from the crate
- Go east and talk to the **fortune-teller**

**At High Street:**

- Go north under the clock tower
- Enter the alley beside the church
- Talk to **Fester Shinetop** (get threatened)
- Enter the jail and try talking to the **prisoner** (bad breath!)
- Enter the **general store**
- Pick up the **sword** and **shovel**
- Ring the bell if the storekeeper isn't present
- Buy the sword and shovel by saying "About this [item]... I want it"
- Ask for **breath mints**
- Return to jail and give prisoner the **mints**

## The Trials

You can complete these in any order, though doing thievery last means missing a scene with Elaine.

### Treasure-Huntery

- Go to the fork in the forest
- Look at the **map** you bought (these are directions, not dance steps)
- Follow the directions: **back, left, right, left, right, back, right, left, back**
- "Back" means toward the back of the screen (north)
- You should reach a screen with red flowers across the bottom
- Go east, look at the plaque and marker
- Use **shovel** on the X
- Look at your prize: a **T-shirt**
- Return to the island map

Pirates will now wander the map for swordfighting, but you need training first.

ðŸ’¡ **For Your Amusement**: (EGA version only) Look at the stump in the forest.

### Thievery

**Preparing the Sleeping Potion:**

- Go to the fork, then north one screen
- Pick the yellow **Caniche endormi flowers**
- Use **flowers** on the **meat** (or use both in the Scumm Bar cooking pot)

**At the Governor's Mansion:**

- Leave forest going east twice
- Go to High Street, then west past the jail
- Follow path to the Governor's mansion
- Give **drugged meat** to the piranha poodles
- Open door and enter
- Optionally take the **vase**
- Go through first doors on your left
- Watch the entertaining sequence with Elaine

**Getting the File:**

- When you say you need a file, leave the mansion
- Go to the jail and talk to **Otis**
- When he mentions needing rat repellent, give him the **gopher repellent**
- Use the **carrot cake** to find the **file**

**Stealing the Idol:**

- Return to mansion and go back into the room
- Watch more amusing scenes with Elaine
- Try to talk to her (stumble over your words)
- Leave the mansion - Fester appears and tosses you in the ocean
- Pick up the **idol** underwater
- Climb ladder and pick up a **sword**
- Enjoy romantic cutscene with Elaine on the dock

ðŸ’¡ **For Your Amusement**: Look at Elaine's poster after stealing the idol. Wait underwater for 10 real minutes (save first - there's an achievement for this!).

### Swordfighting

**Getting to Captain Smirk:**

- Go to the island map
- Find the grey dot labeled "bridge" west of the bay lights
- Talk to the **troll** and give him the **fish** (red herring!)
- Go to the house at the end of the path
- Talk to **Captain Smirk**
- When he asks about training, tell him you do have what it takes
- Keep saying "I do so!" until he agrees
- Pay for training and show him your sword

**Learning Insults:**
You need to encounter wandering pirates on the map to learn insult/response pairs:

- Don't click directly on pirates - click near them and let them run into you
- Start fights with "My name is Guybrush Threepwood. Prepare to die!"
- Keep trying new insults until you learn the proper responses
- You need to win at least 3 fights to challenge the Sword Master

## Part II: The Journey

### Gathering Your Crew

**Otis - Jailbreak:**

- Return to village after Elaine is kidnapped
- Talk to the lookout and read LeChuck's note
- Go to Scumm Bar - it's empty except for the sobbing cook
- Collect **5 empty mugs** from both rooms of the bar
- Get **grog** from the kitchen barrel
- Transport grog to jail by pouring between mugs as they melt
- You have seconds before each mug melts, about 50 seconds total before failure
- Use **melting mug** on the jail cell lock
- Watch Otis leave

ðŸ’¡ **For Your Amusement**: Look at Elaine's poster after kidnapping, pour grog on the other cell lock, try recruiting the Men of Low Moral Fiber.

**Meathook:**

- Go to "shore" on the island map (top right center)
- Climb the ladder using the pole
- Use **rubber chicken** on the cable to zip across
- Tell Meathook the Governor's been kidnapped
- Say you want to get a crew together
- Open the small door when he tells you to
- Interact with the parrot (use any verb)
- Return via rubber chicken

**Carla:**

- Go to the Sword Master's location
- Tell her the Governor's been kidnapped
- She'll join immediately

### The Sea Monkey

**Getting Credit:**

- Go to the "lights" (Used Ship Emporium)
- Tell **Stan** you don't have much to spend
- Look at the Sea Monkey, ask about credit
- Exit conversation ("On second thought...")
- Go to the general store and ask about a note of credit
- Say you have a job, watch him open the safe carefully
- Try any occupation (all will fail)
- Watch him close the safe, then ask about the Sword Master again
- While he's gone, open the safe using the combination you observed
- Take the **note of credit**

**Buying the Ship:**

- Return to Stan with the credit
- Ask to see the cheap ship again
- Make offers: 2000, then 5000
- Say "Forget it," then "Well, maybe you're right"
- Repeat, then offer 5000 again - he'll accept
- Watch the cutscene at the dock

ðŸ’¡ **For Your Amusement**: Read Stan's office door note after buying the ship.

### Preparing the Voodoo Potion

**Gathering Ingredients:**

- In captain's cabin: open drawer, get **feather pen** and **ink**, look at book
- Open cabinet with **small key**, get **chest** containing **cinnamon sticks** and paper
- Go below deck to hold: get **gunpowder** and **rope** from barrel
- Open chest for **wine**
- In galley, put these items in the cooking pot:
  - **Rubber chicken** (chicken)
  - **Breath mints** (mint)
  - **Ink** (squid ink)
  - **Cereal** (vitamins)
  - **Gunpowder** (brimstone)
  - **Wine** (blood)
  - **Jolly Roger** (skull)
  - **Cinnamon sticks**

**Creating Fire:**

- After the explosion, click anywhere to stand up
- Use one of these on the fire: map, T-shirt, Stan's card, feather pen, or dusty book
- Take **small pot** from counter
- Get more **gunpowder** from hold
- On deck: use **rope** on cannon, **gunpowder** on nozzle, **flaming mass** on rope
- Use **pot** to get in the cannon and fire yourself to Monkey Island

## Part III: Under Monkey Island

### Exploring the Island

**Initial Setup:**

- After cutscene with Herman, click anywhere to get unstuck
- Look at paper on tree, pick up **banana**
- Enter jungle and go northwest to find the fort
- Get **spyglass**, **rope**, **gunpowder**, and **cannon ball**
- Push the cannon (Herman will appear and leave)

**Opening the Dam:**

- Go to the river fork and get note from under small rock
- Use **gunpowder** on dam
- Ignite with one of these methods:
  - Open **spyglass**, use **lens** with sun
  - Use **flint rock** with **staple remover**
  - Use **flint rock** with **cannon ball**
  - Use **flint rock** with **unopened spyglass**
  - Use **flint rock** with **compass**

**Getting Down the Cliff:**

- Go to pond, look at note, get **rope** from corpse
- Go to crack near landing spot
- Find "strong branch" in the greenery
- Use **rope** on branch, climb down
- Use second **rope** on stump, climb down again
- Get **oars** and climb back up
- Go to beach and use **oars** with rowboat

### The Cannibals

**Meeting Them:**

- Sail to north lobe of island
- Read note on beach, enter jungle
- Go to cannibals' village, then two screens west
- Get **bananas** from bowl
- Return east and get caught

**Escaping:**

- In guest hut, read memo and get **skull**
- Notice loose board underneath
- Pick up **board** and go down hole
- Watch cannibal conversation from background
- Return to boat

**The Monkey and Catapult:**

- Go to south beach of east peninsula, get **bottle**
- Go to river fork, up footholds, look at note
- Pull "primitive art" twice (log faces screen)
- Go up next footholds, push rock (Herman appears)
- After talking, push rock to create bridge
- Go down and collect **bananas** knocked from tree
- Find the monkey (it moves around) and give it all 5 **bananas**
- Go to clearing on east peninsula with monkey following
- Pull nose on left totem pole, walk away
- Monkey will copy you and pull it down
- Enter monkey head area and get **wimpy little idol**

**Trading with Cannibals:**

- Return to village, get caught again
- Say "Don't eat me! I'll give you anything!"
- Give them the **idol**
- Go west into hut and get **banana picker**
- Give **banana picker** to Herman (who's conveniently here)
- Leave and return to village
- Say you're looking for somebody
- Give them the **leaflet** ("How to get Ahead in Navigating")
- Receive **Head of the Navigator** with ghost-invisible necklace

ðŸ’¡ **For Your Amusement**: Walk to the sun at river fork, get caught by cannibals 5+ times to see increasing security, use catapult wrong to sink Sea Monkey, walk off eastern cliff edge.

### Inside the Monkey Head

**Navigation:**

- Return to monkey head and use **key** in ear
- Enter mouth and follow path north, across bridge, east
- Use **Head of the Navigator** - it will point directions
- Follow where it points, walking into each screen before checking again
- Make 6 correct moves total to reach LeChuck's ship overlook

**Getting the Necklace:**

- Talk to head, ask for necklace
- Either keep pleading "Pretty please with sugar on top" or threaten him
- Use **necklace** to become invisible to ghosts
- Go down to ship

### LeChuck's Ship

**Exploring the Ship:**

- Enjoy the Ghost Ship Shuffle music
- Open west door - don't get too close to LeChuck!
- Use **magnetic compass** on key hanging on wall
- Go down hatch to hold
- Try to pick up chicken to get **feather**
- Look at glowing crate
- In crew quarters, use **ghost feather** on sleeping ghost's feet (twice)
- Pick up **jug o' grog**
- Use **key** on locked hatch, go down
- Use **grog** on dish - rat drinks and falls over
- Get **grease** from vat
- Return to deck

**Getting the Root:**

- Use **grease** on east door, open it
- Take **ghost tools** from wall
- Return to hold and use **tools** on crate
- Look at crate to get **root**
- Leave ship (auto-jump to cannibal village)
- Watch voodoo ceremony cutscene
- Leave village (auto-jump back to ship area)

**The Root Beer:**

- Ship is gone - talk to **Bob**
- Your crew arrives (or Herman if you sank the Sea Monkey)
- Get transported back to MÃªlÃ©e Island docks

ðŸ’¡ **For Your Amusement**: Wander underground until descriptions become "cool" instead of "creepy", use mean dialogue options on Bob to see different ending.

## Part IV: Guybrush Kicks Butt

### The Final Confrontation

**Back on MÃªlÃ©e Island:**

- Try going east - get chased back by ghost pirate
- Use **root beer** automatically on first pirate
- Go east and encounter second pirate
- Use **root beer** again
- Go north under clock tower
- Open church door and stop the wedding

**Fighting LeChuck:**

- Choose any dialogue (root beer options reveal it's jammed)
- Let LeChuck punch you around the island
- When you crash into Stan's grog machine, notice green bottle falls out
- After LeChuck pulls you out, stand up quickly
- Pick up **root beer bottle** and use on LeChuck
- If you're too slow, he'll hit you again - just try again
- You can let him hit you to choose your victory location

**The End:**

- Enjoy final romantic scene with Elaine
- Ending varies based on whether you sank the Sea Monkey
- Roll credits and bask in your victory!
