# King's Quest I Walkthrough

**Maximum total points:** 158

## Starting Area

### Outside Castle

- After the intro, go west

### Rock (7 points)

- Stand behind the rock and **PUSH ROCK** (2 points)  
  ⚠️ _Warning: If you're in front of the rock, it'll crush you_
- **LOOK IN HOLE** and **GET DAGGER** (5 points)
- Walk one screen west to a lake and **GET PEBBLES** (1 point)
- Go 1 screen east and one north to the big tree

---

## Early Item Collection

### Big Tree (8 points)

- Go to the base of the tree and **CLIMB TREE** (2 points)
- Walk across the branch (it's easy if you use your computer mouse) and **GET EGG** (6 points)
- Walk off the branch to fall down (you'll survive) and go 1 screen east to the carrot field

### Carrot Field (2 points)

- **GET CARROT** (2 points)
- Go east twice

### Fairy Godmother (Optional)

_Note: Fairy godmother may appear and cast a protective spell on Graham. This spell only works temporarily, but you don't really need it._

- Go one north to the clover field

### Clover Field (2 points)

- **GET CLOVER** (2 points)
- Walk one screen north and 2 west to the screen with the bowl

### Bowl (4 points)

- **GET BOWL** (3 points) and **LOOK BOWL** (1 point)
- _The bowl has the word "fill" on its bottom_
- Walk one screen south

### Elf Encounter (3 points)

- Leave and return to screen until an elf shows up
- Walk to elf and **TALK TO ELF** to get a ring (3 points)
- _The ring has the power to turn you invisible when worn_
- Walk one screen north, and one west towards the walnut tree

### Walnut Tree (6 points)

- **GET WALNUT** (3 points) and **OPEN WALNUT** (3 points)
- _It is pure gold!_
- **SAVE** the game because we'll walk through a dangerous area
- Walk 2 screens west to the witch's house

---

## Witch's House Sequence

### Witch's House (14 points)

- Walk up near door and **EAT HOUSE** (2 points)
- If the response is _"Yum! The house tastes even better than it looks!"_, then the witch is not home and you can **OPEN DOOR**
- Otherwise, if you hear the witch inside, leave the screen, reenter, and eat house again until you get the "Yum!" response
- Enter the bedroom: **GET NOTE** (1 point) and **READ NOTE** (2 points)
- _The note reads: "Sometimes it is wise to think backwards"_
- When the witch comes home and stands by the cauldron, sneak behind her and **PUSH WITCH** in the cauldron (7 points)
- **OPEN CUPBOARD** (2 points) and **GET CHEESE** (2 points)
- Exit the house and go 2 north

---

## Village Area

### Poor People's House (8 points)

- Enter the house, **FILL BOWL** (2 points) and **GIVE BOWL** (3 points)
- When the man offers his fiddle, **GET FIDDLE** (3 points)
- Exit the house and walk one north and one west

### Stump (7 points)

- **LOOK IN STUMP** (1 point), **GET POUCH** (3 points) and **LOOK IN POUCH** (3 points)
- _It's filled with diamonds!_
- Go north to the corral

### Corral (5 points)

- **SAVE** the game (in case the goat escapes)
- **OPEN GATE** and enter the corral
- Walk near the goat and **SHOW CARROT**, so the goat follows (5 points)
- Leave the corral and walk one west and 3 south towards the bridge with the goat following you

---

## Bridge and Gnome

### Bridge (4 points)

- Start to cross bridge and a bridge troll will appear and block your path
- The goat will spring into action, so move aside so the goat can dispose of the troll (4 points)
- Cross the bridge to get to the gnome

### Gnome (9 points)

- **TALK TO GNOME** and guess his name: **NIKSTLITSELPMUR** (9 points)
- _This is "Rumplestiltskin" spelled backwards, as the note in the witch's house hinted at_
- _Note: This puzzle has been simplified from the original AGI version where the letters were also reversed to form IFNKOVHGROGHPRM_
- The gnome gives you magic beans
- Walk south twice

---

## Beanstalk and Giant's Castle

### Cloverfield - Beanstalk (4 points)

- Stand right of the clovers and **PLANT BEANS** - a giant beanstalk will grow (2 points)
- **CLIMB** beanstalk to the land in the clouds (2 points)
- **Save frequently!**

### Clouds - Giant's Area (17 points)

- Walk east twice, south, east twice
- **LOOK IN HOLE** of the tree and **GET SLING** (2 points)
- **SAVE** the game
- Walk north and west and stand behind the lower right tree
- Hide until giant falls asleep (7 points)
- _Alternative: You could USE SLING ON GIANT and kill him, but that yields only 3 points_
- **GET CHEST** (8 points)
- Go right into the cave

### Cave

- **WEAR RING**, **RUB RING** to turn invisible and go down the path to the exit
- _If you don't wear the ring, the little gnome will get you on the path and pick your pockets_

---

## Underground Adventure

### Well Area (14 points)

- Go 2 west and 1 south to the well
- Walk behind well and **CUT ROPE** (2 points)
- Walk to crank and **LOWER ROPE**. **CLIMB ROPE** and move down (2 points)
- **DROP** in the water and **FILL BUCKET** (2 points)
- **DIVE** (4 points), move down, left and up to the surface and **GET OUT**. Then go left (4 points)
- Go 1 screen west and 1 north to exit the dragon cave (2 points)

### Dragon Battle (14 points)

- Move towards dragon until he faces Graham and **THROW WATER** (6 points)
- **GET MIRROR** (8 points)

---

## Final Sequence

### Bird Transport (3 points)

- Stand near the center bottom and **JUMP** to be grabbed by the bird and be flown away (3 points)

### Underground Chambers (20 points)

- Walk west, **GET MUSHROOM** (1 point)
- Walk east and fall in hole
- Walk south, west and **GIVE CHEESE** to the rat (2 points)
- Enter the door and quickly **PLAY FIDDLE** (3 points)
  _The leprechauns will otherwise vanish because of the clover you carry, but you won't receive points_
- Walk further into the throne room. All leprechauns will disappear, driven mad by the music
- **GET SHIELD** (8 points)
- **GET SCEPTRE** (6 points)

### Return to Castle (6 points)

- Walk west up the stairs and to the small doorway
- **EAT MUSHROOM** (3 points) to become tiny and go through the hole
- Go north twice and east twice and **OPEN GATE**. Then enter the castle (3 points)

**THE END - 158/158 points**

---

## Important Tips and Notes

- **Save frequently**, especially before dangerous areas and the giant sequence
- The gnome's name is **NIKSTLITSELPMUR** ("Rumplestiltskin" backwards)
- Always stand **behind** the rock when pushing it to avoid being crushed
- The invisibility ring is crucial for safely navigating the cave
- Wait for the giant to fall asleep naturally for maximum points
- Time your actions carefully with the goat and troll sequence
- The witch's note about "thinking backwards" is key to solving the gnome puzzle
