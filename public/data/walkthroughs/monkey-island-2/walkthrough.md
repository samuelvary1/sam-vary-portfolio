## Introduction

Monkey Island 2: LeChuck's Revenge is widely considered one of the greatest adventure games ever made. It features more complex puzzles than its predecessor, multiple islands to explore, and the introduction of the legendary treasure "Big Whoop."

### Game Overview

- **Multiple Islands**: Explore Scabb Island, Booty Island, and Phatt Island
- **Complex Inventory Puzzles**: More challenging puzzle design than the first game
- **Non-Linear Gameplay**: Many puzzles can be solved in different orders
- **Spitting Contest**: Master the art of loogie launching
- **No Death Rule**: Like its predecessor, you cannot die in this game

## Part I: The Largo Embargo

### Scabb Island Locations

- **Woodtick**: Main town with toll bridge, woodsmith, bar, kitchen, map maker, inn, laundry
- **Beach**: Coastal area with access to other locations
- **Swamp**: Home to the Voodoo Lady
- **Cemetery**: Final resting place of various pirates
- **Peninsula**: Location of Captain Dread's boat

### Getting Rid of Largo

You start with money, but Largo LaGrande will quickly take it all from you. To get rid of him, you need to create a voodoo doll with four specific ingredients.

**Getting the Voodoo Shopping List:**

1. **Peninsula**: Walk to boathouse and talk to **Captain Dread**

   - He'll tell you to get rid of Largo before you can leave the island

2. **Swamp**: Visit the Voodoo Lady
   - Walk to coffin and use it to row to the shack
   - Talk to **Mojo** the voodoo lady
   - Ask about the voodoo doll to get the shopping list
   - You need: **something from the dead**, **something from the head**, **something from the thread**, and **something from the body**

### Collecting Voodoo Ingredients

**1. Something from the Dead (Largo's Ancestor):**

- **Woodtick - Toll Bridge**: Look at the sign and pick it up to get the **shovel**
- **Cemetery**: Look at tombstones to find Largo's ancestor's grave
- Use **shovel** to dig up the appropriate grave
- Get the **bone**

**2. Something from the Body (Largo's Body Fluid):**

- **Woodtick - Wally's Place**: Pick up a **piece of paper** from the pile
- **Woodtick - Bar**: Talk to bartender and mention Largo
- Largo will appear and spit on the wall
- Wait for him to leave, then use **piece of paper** on the spit

**3. Something from the Head (Largo's Hair):**

- **Woodtick - Kitchen Window**: Enter kitchen through the window
- Pick up **knife** from table
- **Woodtick - Inn**: Talk to innkeeper
- Use **knife** on rope to release the alligator
- Innkeeper runs after alligator - enter **Largo's room**
- Pick up **toupee** from the plastic head

**4. Something from the Thread (Largo's Clothing):**

- **Woodtick - Laundry**: Pick up **bucket**
- **Swamp**: Use **bucket** on swamp to get mud
- **Largo's Room**: Close door and put **bucket of mud** on top
- Wait for Largo to enter (gets muddy)
- Follow him to laundry and listen to conversation
- Return to his room, close door, get **laundry ticket** from behind door
- **Laundry**: Give **ticket** to get Largo's clothing

**Creating the Voodoo Doll:**

- **Swamp**: Give all four items to Mojo
- Receive **voodoo doll** and **pins**
- **Largo's Room**: Use **pins** on **voodoo doll**
- Largo gets blasted off the island!

### Escaping Scabb Island

**Getting Captain Dread's Requirements:**

1. **Woodtick - Wally's Place**:

   - Talk to Wally extensively
   - Wait for him to put down his **monocle**, then pick it up

2. **Peninsula**: Give **monocle** to Captain Dread
   - He needs 20 pieces of eight for passage

**Earning Money:**

**Method 1 - Wood Polish Scam:**

- **Laundry**: Talk to pirates and buy **wood polish**
- **Woodsmith**: Buy the wood polish
- **Laundry**: Use **wood polish** on peg leg for pieces of eight
- This doesn't give enough money, so you need a job

**Method 2 - Getting a Job at the Bar:**

_Gathering Materials:_

- **Beach**: Pick up **stick**
- **Swamp**: Pick up **string** at Mojo's place
- **Inn**: Pick up **cheese squiggles** from bowl

_Setting the Trap:_

- **Laundry**: Open box, use **stick** on box
- Use **cheese squiggles** on box
- Use **string** on **stick**
- Wait for rat to eat cheese, pull string to catch **rat**

_Getting the Job:_

- **Kitchen** (enter via window): Put **rat** in pot
- **Bar**: Talk to bartender about stew
- He finds the rat, fires the cook, and hires you
- Receive 420 pieces of eight

3. **Peninsula**: Pay Captain Dread and sail away

## Part II: Four Map Pieces

You now have access to three islands: **Scabb**, **Booty**, and **Phatt**. You need to collect four map pieces from different people.

### Initial Setup

- **Outside the boat**: Pick up **parrot chow**
- Read the book about Big Whoop to learn who has the map pieces

### Map Piece from Rapp Scallion

**Getting the Tools:**

1. **Booty Island - Antique Shop**: Buy the **well polished saw**

2. **Scabb Island - Woodtick**:
   - **Laundry**: Use **saw** on peg leg
   - **Woodsmith**: He's out - pick up **hammer** and **nails**

**Dealing with Stan:**

3. **Booty Island - Stan's Coffin Shop**:
   - Talk to Stan and ask to see a coffin
   - When he gets in, close the coffin (get **hankie**)
   - Close it again and use **hammer** and **nails** to trap him
   - Get **crypt key**

**Finding Rapp's Coffin:**

4. **Phatt Island**:

   - You'll be captured and jailed
   - **Jail**: Open mattress, get **stick**
   - Use **stick** on **bone**, give **bone** to dog
   - Get **key** and use it on jail door
   - Get **envelopes** (contains banana, organ, and personal items)

5. **Library**: Get temporary library card

   - Borrow "Quotations: The Joy of Hex" voodoo book

6. **Mansion**:
   - Open gate, talk to guard (get him to leave)
   - Go upstairs, use any book to swap the governor's book
   - Look at book - contains famous pirate quotes

**Resurrecting Rapp:**

7. **Scabb Island - Cemetery**:

   - Use **crypt key** to open crypt
   - Find Rapp Scallion's coffin using the quotes
   - Look inside and pick up **ash**

8. **Swamp - Mojo's Place**:

   - Give **ash** to Mojo to get **ash-2-life potion**
   - Use voodoo book if needed

9. **Cemetery**: Use **potion** on Rapp's ash

   - Talk to Rapp and get **key** to his weenie hut

10. **Beach**: Use **key** on weenie hut door

    - Turn **knob** to shut off gas

11. **Cemetery**: Talk to Rapp again to get **map piece**

### Map Piece from Young Lindy

**The Spitting Contest:**

1. **Booty Island - Antique Shop**:

   - Map piece costs 6 million pieces of eight
   - Buy the **horn**
   - Talk to **Captain Kate Capsize** and get **leaflet**

2. **Spitting Contest**:
   - Try spitting (you're terrible)
   - Look at flags, try to pick them up
   - Use **horn** to distract everyone
   - Pick up flags and rearrange them
   - Try spitting again (better but not good enough)

**Improving Your Spitting:**

3. **Scabb Island - Bar**:

   - Show **library card** to buy drinks
   - Buy **blue drink** and **yellow drink** (get **crazy straw**)
   - Use **blue drink** on **yellow drink** to make **green drink**

4. **Booty Island - Spit Master**:
   - Use **crazy straw** with **green drink**
   - Enter contest again with thicker spit
   - Spit only when the woman's scarf is flying (windy conditions)
   - Win the contest and get **plaque**

**Getting the Mad Monkey Head:**

5. **Antique Shop**: Sell **plaque** for 6000 pieces of eight

   - Charter Captain Kate's ship

6. **Phatt Island - Library**:

   - Borrow "Great Shipwrecks of the Century"
   - Note the location of the sunken Mad Monkey

7. **Booty Island**:
   - Talk to Captain Kate about treasure hunting
   - Go to the Mad Monkey's location and dive
   - Pick up **head piece** and use anchor to surface
   - Trade **head piece** for **map piece** at antique shop

### Map Piece from Captain Marley

**The Gambling Scam:**

1. **Phatt Island - Alley**:

   - Talk to man and play the game (you'll lose)
   - Notice the little guy always wins
   - Follow him to another alley and observe

2. **Secret Door**:
   - Knock on slot and ask about winning numbers
   - Remember the number of fingers shown
   - Always answer with the previous number shown
   - Return and win the **invitation card**

**Governor Marley's Party:**

3. **Booty Island - Costume Shop**:

   - Give **invitation** to get **costume**

4. **Phatt Island - Mansion**:
   - Wear **costume** and enter party
   - Go to kitchen (side entrance)
   - Try to get fish (get chased out)
   - Pick up **trash can** (chef chases you)
   - Run around mansion and re-enter kitchen
   - Pick up **fish**
   - Enter mansion and pick up **map piece**

**The Great Escape:**

5. **Outside Mansion**:

   - Try to leave (dog and gardener stop you)
   - Talk sweetly to Elaine
   - She throws map piece out window
   - Pick up **oars** and exit

6. **Cliff**: Map piece is stuck on cliff edge

   - Pick up **dog**

7. **Phatt Island - Pier**:

   - Talk to fisherman and bet with him
   - Give him **fish** to get **fishing pole**

8. **Booty Island - Cliff**:
   - Use **fishing pole** on map
   - Bird takes it away to the big tree

### Map Piece from Mister Rogers

**Climbing the Big Tree:**

1. **Big Tree**:

   - Pick up **plank** from hole
   - Use **oar** in first hole and stand on it
   - Oar breaks - get list of needed items in inventory

2. **Scabb Island - Woodsmith**:

   - Give broken **oars** to be fixed

3. **Big Tree Climbing Sequence**:

   - Use **oar** in first hole, stand on it
   - Use **plank** in second hole, stand on it
   - Pick up **oar**, put in third hole, stand on it
   - Pick up **plank**, put in fourth hole
   - Repeat pattern until you reach the top

4. **Tree House**:
   - Look at pile of maps
   - Use **dog** to retrieve **map piece**

**Additional Items Needed Later:**

5. **Big Tree**: Pick up **telescope**

6. **Booty Island - Antique Shop**:
   - Buy **sign** beside parrot
   - Notice **hook** behind sign
   - Use **parrot chow** with **hook**
   - Buy **mirror**

**Final Preparations:**

7. **Scabb Island - Bar**:

   - Use **banana** on **metronome**
   - Pick up **monkey**

8. **Phatt Island**:
   - Use **leaflet** on poster to free Captain Kate
   - Enter jail, use **key** to free her
   - Get **envelope** with **bottle of near-grog**

### The Cottage Challenge

**Getting to the Cottage:**

1. **Waterfall**:
   - Walk to path above waterfall
   - Use **monkey** on pump (stops water flow)
   - Enter cave to reach cottage

**Winning the Drinking Contest:**

2. **Cottage**:
   - Talk to man and try challenge (you'll lose)
   - Try again: pick up **mug**
   - Use **mug** on tree to empty it
   - Use **mug** with **bottle of near-grog**
   - Win the contest!

**Getting the Final Map Piece:**

3. **Cottage Preparation**:

   - Open window first
   - Use **mirror** on mirror frame
   - Go outside and use **telescope** on grotesque statue
   - Sun ray burns hole in brick

4. **Trapdoor**:
   - Push brick (now burns through)
   - Look at skeleton and pick up **map piece**

### Assembling the Map

1. **Phatt Island - Library**:

   - Open lighthouse model and get **lens**

2. **Scabb Island - Wally's Place**:

   - Give **lens** to Wally
   - Give him all four **map pieces**
   - He asks you to run an errand

3. **Swamp - Mojo's Place**:

   - Get **juju bag** containing **love bomb** and **matches**

4. **Woodtick - Wally's Place**:

   - Wally has been captured by LeChuck!

5. **Swamp**: Notice box near swamp
   - Look at it, open it, and use it to travel to LeChuck's Fortress

## Part III: LeChuck's Fortress

### Initial Exploration

- Walk right to find lots of signs (don't enter yet)
- Walk to right tunnel and look at jail
- Talk to **Wally** in the jail
- Walk back to signs and enter back tunnels

### Bone Puzzle

- Look at piece of paper in inventory
- Push bones according to verses on the paper
- Do this 4 times, one for each verse
- Find huge door and open it (reveals doggie door)
- Pick up **jail key** - it's a trap!

### Escape Sequence

You're now hanging in the air. You can only use your mouth:

1. Use **crazy straw** on **green drink** to spit
2. Spit on two locations to adjust angles:
   - **Pan** (below Wally)
   - **Shield** (to your right)
3. Adjust angles so spit bounces from shield to pan to candle
4. This turns off light and you escape
5. Use **box of matches** to light up the area

## Part IV: Dinky Island

### Beach Exploration

Talk to **Herman Toothrot** extensively until you get the right responses.

**Collecting Items:**

- Pick up **crowbar**
- Pick up **martini glass**
- Use **glass** with ocean, then with still to get **distilled water**
- Pick up **bottle** in ocean
- Use **bottle** on rock to break it
- Open **barrel** to get **cracker**
- Give **cracker** to **parrot** and note what it says

### Jungle Adventure

1. **Big Tree Area**:

   - Walk left to big tree
   - Use **broken bottle** on bag
   - Get **cracker mix**
   - Use **distilled water** on **cracker mix** to get two crackers

2. **Following the Parrot**:

   - Walk right three times to reach pond
   - Pick up **rope**
   - Use **crowbar** on box to get **dynamite**
   - Follow parrot's directions to find it
   - Give **cracker** to parrot, get new directions
   - Find parrot again, give second **cracker**
   - Find the big X

3. **The Big X**:
   - Use **shovel** on big X
   - Use **matches** with **dynamite**
   - Use **dynamite** with hole to blow it up
   - Use **crowbar** with **rope**
   - Use **crowbar and rope** with metal rod

### Underground Complex

Find the light switch in darkness and turn it on. LeChuck appears and zaps you to random rooms. Learn the room layouts and work quickly:

**First Aid Room:**

- Open trash can to get **2 surgical gloves**
- Open drawer to get **hypodermic syringe**
- Pick up **skull**

**Box Room:**

- Open all accessible boxes
- Pick up **root beer**, **voodoo doll**, **balloon**

**Interacting with LeChuck:**

- Give **hankie** to LeChuck (he blows his nose and returns it)

**Grog Machine Room:**

- Use **helium tank** with **surgical gloves** and **balloon**
- Use coin return to get **coin**
- Wait for LeChuck to enter and bend down for coin
- Pick up his **underwear**

**Elevator:**

- Enter elevator (balloons make you light enough)
- Wait for LeChuck, use lever to go up
- Door shuts and you get his **beard**

### Final Confrontation

1. Use **juju bag** with **voodoo doll**, **beard**, **underwear**, **used hankie**, and **skull**
2. Mix ingredients to create **LeChuck voodoo doll**
3. Find LeChuck and use **syringe** on **voodoo doll**
4. He'll run away - chase him down
5. Enjoy the ending sequence!
