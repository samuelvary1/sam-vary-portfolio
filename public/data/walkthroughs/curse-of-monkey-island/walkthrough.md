## Introduction

The Curse of Monkey Island represents the visual and audio pinnacle of the classic Monkey Island series. Featuring Disney-quality hand-drawn animation, full voice acting (including Dominic Armato as Guybrush), and memorable music, this game combines modern presentation with classic LucasArts adventure game design.

### Game Features

- **Hand-drawn Animation**: Gorgeous cartoon-style visuals throughout
- **Full Voice Acting**: Complete voice work for all characters
- **Two Difficulty Modes**: Regular and Mega Monkey for extra challenge
- **Insult Sword Fighting**: Enhanced system from previous games
- **Musical Numbers**: Memorable songs including "A Pirate I Was Meant to Be"

## Part I: The Demise of the Zombie Pirate LeChuck

### First Steps

You begin as a prisoner in the hold of LeChuck's pirate ship.

1. **Getting Started**:

   - Pick up the **ramrod** from the wall
   - Talk to the small pirate **Bloodnose** (who is actually Wally from MI2)
   - Tell him he's "a failure as a pirate" and to "snap out of it"
   - Annoy him until he begins to cry
   - When Wally throws his **fake hook** to the floor, pick it up

2. **Cannon Battle**:

   - Use the **cannon** to start the battle
   - Shoot all the skeleton boats with your cannon
   - Optionally try shooting the fort as well

3. **Getting Equipment**:

   - Go to the **window**
   - Optionally talk to **Murray the Skull** or push him into water with ramrod
   - Attach the **hook** to the **ramrod**
   - Pick up the **skeleton arm** floating in the water with the ramrod-hook combination
   - The arm has a **cutlass** attached to it

4. **Escape**:

   - Go back inside
   - Cut the rope holding the cannon with the **sword**
   - Fire the **cannon** to escape

5. **After the Crash**:
   - Pick up the **purse of wooden nickels** from the floor
   - Get the huge **diamond ring** under the purse
   - Cut a hole in the porthole with the **ring**
   - The ship sinks and you reach shore where Elaine is waiting

**In the Quicksand:**

**At the Chicken Restaurant:**

### A Map and a Boat

**Getting the Map:**

- Go to lemonade stand and swap **mugs**
- Buy **lemonade** and take the **pitcher**

**Escape Sequence:**

## Part III: Three Sheets to the Wind

### Sea Battle

**Initial Setup:**

- **Captain Rottingham** steals your map to Blood Island
- Enjoy the hilarious musical performance "A Pirate I Was Meant to Be"
- To stop the song, choose: "We'll surely avoid scurvy, if we all eat an orange"
- **McMutton** asks about difficulty - choose your sea battle preference

**Ship Combat and Insult Sword Fighting:**

- Examine the **map** to see available targets
- Choose boats marked with "?" to attack
- After ship combat, engage in sword fighting
- Learn **insults** and **comebacks** that rhyme
- Start fights with insults you don't know responses to
- After learning, return to **Puerto Pollo** to buy bigger cannons
- Continue until you have the best cannons and know all replies

**Final Battle:**

- Attack **Captain Rottingham**
- He uses different insults - you must defend constantly
- Use the **Rottingham-specific comebacks** (see reference tables below)
- After victory, get your map back and sail to **Blood Island**

## Part IV: The Bartender, the Thieves, his Aunt, and her Lover

### Bloody Island

**Crash Landing:**

- Wake up on beach after the crash
- Talk to **Haggis** until you learn he needs **tar**
- Pick up **bottle** from beach
- ðŸ’Ž **Mega Monkey**: Bite bottle open to get **cork**

**At the Hotel:**

- Talk to **Madame Xima** about tarot reading
- Ask to see your future until she turns five **death cards**
- Get the **cards**
- Get **pillow** from chair
- Talk to man with hangover
- Take **recipe book** near mirror
- Find hangover cure recipe: **dog hair**, **egg**, and **pepper**

**Gathering Ingredients:**

- **Graveyard**: Get **chisel** and **mallet**
- **Dog Area**:
  - ðŸŸ¢ **Easy**: Get **biscuits** and give to dog
  - ðŸ’Ž **Mega Monkey**: Give **chicken restaurant biscuit** to dog
  - Get **dog hair**
- **Windmill**: Pick up **pepper**
- **Beach**: Put **pillow** on rocks, hit tree with **mallet**, get **egg**

### Dangerous Drinking Habits

**Making the Hangover Cure:**

- Give **pepper**, **egg**, and **dog hair** to bartender
- He makes cure, drinks some, gives you the rest
- Talk to him about the Goodsoup family

**Joining the Goodsoup Family:**

- Go to back room, use **chisel** on **cheese**, get **refrigerator magnet**
- Order drink with umbrella from bartender
- Open **hangover cure** with **chisel**
- Pour **hangover cure** into **drink**
- Drink the mixture (Warning: Don't try this at home!)

**In the Crypt (while "dead"):**

- Open coffin lid with **chisel**, get **nails**
- Open other coffin with **chisel**
- Meet **Stan** (accidentally nailed in from MI2)
- Talk to Stan and get **business card**
- Leave tomb, go out of graveyard, return to tomb
- Get **life insurance**

**Back at the Hotel:**

- Go upstairs to first door
- Hit **nail** on wall with **mallet**
- Get **nail** from floor
- ðŸ’Ž **Mega Monkey**:
  - Pick up **painting**, cut with **scissors**
  - Use **painting frames** on first door
  - Open far door with **business card**

**The Portrait Gallery:**

- Go to far door
- Open **bed** and nail it down with collected **nails**
- Get **book** from skeleton and read it
- Tell bartender you're part of Goodsoup family
- Share information from the book
- Go back upstairs, enter first door, close it
- Look out **porthole** until **Goodsoup** sees you in gallery
- He accepts you as family member

**Lighthouse Preparation:**

- ðŸ’Ž **Mega Monkey**: Get **mirror** from wall, use cut **face** on mirror frame
- Take **jar** and use **chisel** on jar lid
- Go to windmill
- ðŸ’Ž **Mega Monkey**: Use **umbrella** on windmill blades
- Fill **jar** from barrel
- Go to clearing, catch **fireflies** in jar, close lid
- Go to lighthouse, put **firefly jar** on lantern stand
- ðŸ’Ž **Mega Monkey**: Use **mirror** on mirror

### Volcano Eruption

**At the Cannibal Village:**

- Take **block of tofu** and **measuring cup**
- Cut **tofu** with **chisel** to make mask
- Talk to **Lemonhead** (vegetarian cannibal)
- Wear **tofu block** and attend ceremony
- Throw **cheese** in volcano (volcano is lactose intolerant!)

**Getting the Ring:**

- Return to hotel, use remaining **cheese** in pot on barbecue
- After cheese melts, take **pot** to **Haggis** for **hand lotion**
- Go to clearing, use **lotion** on cursed ring
- Take **ring** from Elaine's finger

**Return to the Dead:**

- Get another drink, mix with **hangover cure**, drink it
- Wake up in Goodsoup family crypt
- Talk to **ghost** on left
- Optionally look through crack in wall for fun
- Go left, take **crowbar**, approach crack
- **Murray** tries to scare you - pick him up
- Look through crack with Murray

**Mort's Challenge:**

- ðŸ’Ž **Mega Monkey**: Use **paste** with **skeleton arm**
- Talk to **Mort**
- Pick up **lantern** with **skeleton arm**
- Put **lantern** on coffin lid in previous room
- Use **Murray** on **lantern** (he lights it)

### To Skull Island

**Preparing to Leave:**

- After **Mort** opens crypt door, return to hotel back room
- Get **death certificate**
- Go upstairs to far room
- Open boarded hole with **crowbar**
- Use **crowbar** on **bed**
- In crypt, get **ring**
- Go to **Stan's**, show **death certificate**
- Get cash (you're officially dead!)

**The Compass:**

- Go to beach, talk to **ferryman**
- Fill **mug** with sea water
- Magnetize **pin** with **refrigerator magnet**
- Push **pin** into **cork**
- Put **cork/pin combination** in **mug**
- Give makeshift compass to **Lost Welshman**
- Get ride to **Skull Island**

**The Poker Game:**

- Go up mountain, talk to **winch operator**
- Use the "elevator" (fall down shaft)
- Open **umbrella** while falling
- Enter cave and talk to pirates
- Play poker - when you get cards, use **tarot cards** on weak hand
- Win the big **uncursed diamond**
- **King Andre** doesn't like losing - prepare for trouble!

**Saving Elaine:**

- After the fight, return to **Blood Island** clearing
- Attach **diamond** to **ring**
- Put **ring** on Elaine's finger
- Elaine returns to normal!
- Both get captured by LeChuck's skeleton servants

## Part VI: Guybrush Kicks Butt Once Again

### Rollercoaster

**Riding the Coaster:**

- You're on a roller coaster ride with LeChuck chasing
- If LeChuck stops you, don't worry - just continue

**Stop 1**: Pick up **fallen rope** from ground

**Stop 2**: Get **keg of rum**

**Stop 3**:

- ðŸŸ¢ **Easy**: Get **flask of oil**
- ðŸ’Ž **Mega Monkey**: Open **lantern**, blow it out, get **flask of oil**

**Stop 4**:

- Go up icy path
- Use **keg** on **monkey**
- Use **oil** on **rope**
- Attach **rope** to **keg**
- When **LeChuck** appears, use **pepper** on him

**Final Victory**: LeChuck is defeated once and for all!
