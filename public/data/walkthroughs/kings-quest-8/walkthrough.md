## Overview

King's Quest VIII: Mask of Eternity marks a dramatic departure from the traditional adventure game format, introducing 3D action-RPG elements to the series. Play as Connor, a tanner who must restore the Mask of Eternity to save the land from an evil curse.

### Game Features

- **3D Action-RPG Gameplay**: Real-time combat with swords, crossbows, and magic
- **Experience System**: Kill enemies to gain experience and increase stats
- **Equipment Management**: Collect weapons, armor, and magical items
- **Multiple Realms**: Seven distinct worlds to explore
- **Save System**: 51 save slots - use 7 per realm for best organization

### Essential Tips

üí° **Save Frequently**: Use 7 saves per realm and save before difficult encounters
‚öîÔ∏è **Kill Everything**: All creatures give experience points - even chickens!
üéØ **Combat Strategy**: Use ranged weapons when possible, back up while fighting
üîç **Search Everything**: Break barrels, open chests, click on wall panels

## General Information

### Combat System

- **Weapon Selection**: Click weapon icons to equip, ALT to put away, DELETE to switch
- **Experience Bar**: Blue bar fills with kills - full bar = level up + stat increases
- **No Strafing**: Major limitation - cannot sidestep attacks
- **CTRL Key**: Interact with environment without putting weapon away

### Healing Items (Center of Interface)

- **Mushrooms**: 5 health points (plentiful, pick up everywhere)
- **Crystals**: 50 health points (moderate healing)
- **Sacred Water**: 100 health points (powerful healing)
- **Elixir of Life**: Full health restoration (rare, use sparingly)

### Magic Potions (Bottom Interface Slots)

- **Shield Potion** (Square/Amber): Temporary damage reduction
- **Strength Potion** (Horn): Increased weapon damage
- **Reveal Potion** (Red): Shows invisible objects/enemies
- **Invisibility Potion** (Green): Temporary stealth

### Strategic Guidelines

- **Collect Rocks**: Essential for triggering pressure plates and traps
- **Healing Springs**: Found in most realms - use instead of wasting potions
- **Search Corpses**: Click different areas to find all dropped items
- **Break Everything**: Barrels and vases contain valuable items
- **Distance Combat**: Kill dangerous enemies from range while backing up

## Kingdom of Daventry

**Starting Location**: Sarah's House

### Sarah's House

- **Enter** and **take mushrooms** from table
- **Take amber shield potion** from shelf near beds
- **Look under bed** for crystal
- **Take kettle broth** only if health is low (don't waste it)

### Outside Sarah's House

- **Take mushrooms** from left side of house
- **Equip fists** and **punch the goblin** on right side
- **Search goblin** for items and **take coins** from corner

### Connor's House Area

- **Punch bull's-eye target** in field (one experience increase only)
- **Punch target** in enclosed side yard (another experience boost)
- **Take 3 mushrooms** left of entry
- **Avoid running goblin** for now

### Connor's House Interior

- **Enter back room** and **take dagger** from table
- **Take kettle broth** if health low
- **Open jar** in corner for **35 coins**
- **Exit** and **kill nearby goblin** with dagger

### Following the Raven

- **Click on raven** at fence post, then **follow** it south
- Go **west** past mausoleum and church
- ‚ö†Ô∏è **Danger**: Spriggan with crossbow between structures - run past quickly
- **Talk to half-frozen wizard** - he gives you a **magic map** (press TAB to view)

### Mausoleum Raid

- Go **south to cliff** overlooking mausoleum roof
- **üíæ SAVE GAME** before attempting jump
- **Line up** corner of cliff with mausoleum roof corner
- **Run and jump** to roof (END key toggles run/walk, INSERT for jumping)
- **Kill Spriggan** inside and **take crossbow**

### Farmhouse Area

- **Enter farmhouse** north of mausoleum
- **Take mushrooms** from table basket
- **Take leather boots** next to bed
- **Take coins** from shelf and **25 more coins** from chest
- **Kill chickens** with crossbow for experience (optional but fun!)
- **Kill monster** near petrified farmer
- **Kill zombies** behind farmhouse

### Village Exploration

#### Farm Stand

- **Take mushrooms** from all three baskets
- **Eat fish and fowl** if health low

#### Fountain

- **Drink** if health down
- Continue northwest to tavern

#### Tavern

- **Take leather gloves** from behind counter (armor rating increase)
- **Take coins** from chest on shelf
- **Drink mugs** to see Connor get dizzy
- **Go upstairs** and **take healing crystals** from large chest
- **Break barrels** on side of tavern for potions

### The Bridge and Windmill

- **Head west** toward bridge with crossbow ready
- **Kill tough Spriggan** with rapid firing
- **Cross bridge** and go to intersection
- **Turn left (south)** toward windmill on hill

#### Windmill Sequence

- **üíæ SAVE** before approaching
- **Kill hopping Spriggan** at door, then another inside
- **Push hay bale** under axe stuck in roof beam
- **Jump on bale** and **take axe**
- **Exit** with axe ready and **drink shield potion**
- **Kill Henchman** and **take everything** he drops

### The Mill Puzzle

- **Go to mill** and note Connor wanting rope and hook
- **Walk around** to tall tree and **chop it down** (stops water wheel)
- **Re-enter mill** and **jump onto milling area**
- **Jump to balcony** (or try back-flip if too difficult)
- **Take rope and hook**

### Kavanagh's House and Falls

- **Enter house** and **click hobby horse** and **drawings**
- **Take mushrooms** from shelf basket
- **Go to outhouse** with axe and **kill occupant**
- **Jump fence** and go **north to falls**

#### Waterfall Cave

- **Use rope and hook** on wall when yellow arrow appears
- **Climb up** and **push stone blocks** off table (stops part of water)
- **Climb down** with rope and hook
- **Enter revealed tunnel** to Castle Daventry

### Castle Daventry

- **Follow zigzag tunnel** and **click candle** to open secret door
- **Enter dining hall** and **click portrait** of King Graham
- **Push portrait** and **take brass key** from niche
- **Take ashes** from burned torch in next room
- **Enter throne room** and **examine statues** and **swirling mirror**

### Eastern Exploration

- **Return to Daventry sign** and go **east**
- **Kill 5 zombies** on path and **take dropped items**
- **Kill Spriggan** guarding mausoleum with bow
- **Break door** with axe and **enter tomb**
- **Push sarcophagus lid** and **take hero ring**

### Castle Keep Assault

- **Enter keep** with axe ready
- **Kill 2 Spriggans** inside entrance passages
- **Climb ramp** and **kill Spriggan** at top left
- **Kill another Spriggan** on this level
- **Climb to lookout** and **kill tough guard**
- **Take leather breastplate** and other items

#### Trap Disarmament

- **Pull three chains** (one in each small room, one single, one double)
- This disarms traps in back hallway
- **Use brass key** on locked door to enter **Teleporter room** (don't use yet!)

### The Alchemist's House

- **Go east through narrow pass** to find unicorn-beast
- **Talk to unicorn** repeatedly until nothing new
- **Break boards** on Alchemist's house with axe
- **Take scroll** (Permanent Spell of Might recipe)
- **Take Elixir of Life** and **Potion of Shield** from shelf

### Church and Sacred Flame

- **Use rope and hook** on church wall to reach roof
- **Climb down** through hole with rope and hook
- **Take burning candle** (sacred flame) from side table
- **Push urn** near lectern (opens mausoleum door)
- **Click coins** on offering box for points
- **Return to surface** and **enter opened mausoleum**

### The Dimension Portal

- **Enter swirling light** to meet Shadow Bane
- **Go to wizard** by lake and **talk to him**
- **Give him candle, ring, and ashes** to get **Ring of Illumination**

#### Island House Sequence

- **Swim west** to tall island in lake
- **Use rope and hook** to climb rock structure
- **Enter house** and **take Potion of Invisibility**
- **Click magical quill** to inscribe Jupiter symbol
- **Take parchment** and **use on spinning globe**
- **Take Potion of Reveal** from inside globe
- **Drink red Potion of Reveal** to see hidden chest
- **Take silver bell** from chest

#### Lady of the Lake

- **Use bell** on bell stand structure
- **Strike bell 3 times** with axe
- **Click on Lady of the Lake** to get **Sword of the Lake**
- **Return to mausoleum** and **enter portal** (Ring dispels Shadow Bane)

## Dimension of Death

**Character**: Connor | **Location**: Egyptian-themed realm

### Portal Entry

- **üíæ SAVE GAME** at start of new realm
- **Turn around** and **draw the 5 Egyptian symbols** above portal (need later!)
- **Equip sword** and **kill 2 archer skeletons** ahead
- **Take potions, crystals, coins** from defeated enemies

### The Lever of Life

- Go **left around building** to find pedestal with spinning object
- **Read pedestal** and **take Lever of Life**
- Find **4 narrow pedestals** with flames around the main doors
- **Insert Lever** in each slot and **push to rotate** (extinguishes flames)
- **Doors open** when all 4 flames are out

### Floating Tile Puzzle

- **Enter building** in 3rd person view (F2)
- **Jump 3 times** to far end of platform, **turn left**
- Step on tiles in order matching the 5 Egyptian symbols:
  1. Strange pitchfork symbol
  2. Half circle with small circle inside
  3. Circle with dot
  4. Two back-to-back half circles with line between
- **Talk to Lord Azriel** (gives you a key)

### Northwest Area Exploration

- Go **northwest** past Azriel's altar
- **Break boxes** to reach fallen warrior, **take iron shield piece**
- Other boxes contain mushrooms or skeleton guards for experience
- ‚ö†Ô∏è **Avoid red blood pools** (instant death!)

### Opening the Gate

- Go to **gate between pyramids** and **kill skeletons** with crossbow
- **Use Azriel's key** on gate
- **Kill third skeleton** shooting from distance
- **Don't go east yet** - go **west** instead

### Trap Disarmament

- **Find switch** on south wall before corner and **flip down**
- This disarms 8 arrow skull traps around corner
- **Stay against left wall** while going around corner
- **Shoot urns** on platform, **jump up** and **get items**
- **Flip switch** on platform (disarms fire traps south)

### The Fountain Area

- Continue to large open area with fountain in center
- **Pick up rocks** (need 4 total eventually)
- **Push 4 metal crates** until plates pop up and torches light
- **Talk to Sylph** at fountain about rust and mold protection spell

### The Crying Child

- Go **south** to passage left of tower
- **Enter left room**, **kill 2 skeletons**, **break boxes**
- **Read pedestal** about key to Creation
- Go to **next room** (several ways to enter over metal crate)
- **Talk to crying child** and **push crate** so she can escape

### Black River of Death

- Go **north** along west wall to **Black River** (DON'T step in water!)
- **üíæ SAVE GAME** before dipping shield
- **Dip iron shield piece** in water
- **Enter boathouse** and **talk to boatman** about skull judging

### East Tower (Skeleton Tower)

- Navigate **east** along riverbank killing zombies
- Work way through corridors to reach **East Tower**
- **üíæ SAVE** before climbing (drink shield potion if available)
- **Climb tower** and **force Commander Skeleton** over edge
- **Take Chain Mail** after skeleton falls

### Key Collection

- Return and head **south** through corridors
- **Kill 5 skeletons** guarding key box with crossbow
- **Take square key** from box

### Skeleton Arena

- Navigate to **blue locked door** in south central area
- **Use square key** and **back out to doorway**
- **Rapid fire crossbow** at 6 fighting skeletons
- **Take circular gold key** and other valuables

### Central Tower Pressure Plates

- Navigate to **Central Tower** area
- **Talk to wounded man** (mentions Hammer for crossing river)
- **Collect rock** and kill Weeper from distance
- At tower: **depress 4 corner pressure plates** with rocks or zombie bodies
- **Climb tower** and **read pedestal**
- **Cross bridge** to get another rock

### The Four Halls

Navigate to fountain - 4 shrine portals now active:

#### Hall of Respite (Northwest Portal)

- **üíæ SAVE** before entering
- **Kill skeleton archers** with crossbow
- **Jump blocks** carefully to cross river
- **Take shield potion** from statue
- **Take 7th skull from left** in skull cabinet
- **Push column** into river and **jump across** using it

#### Hall of War (Northeast Portal)

- **üíæ SAVE** (high health required)
- **Kill archers**, **cross bridge** on right rail
- **Jump gap** and continue to anvil
- **Take potion of strength** and **put skull** on headless statue
- **Take Feather of Truth**

#### Hall of Immortality (Southwest Portal)

- **Look at huge heart**
- **Take red potion of reveal**
- **Exit immediately**

#### Hall of Justice (Southeast Portal)

- **Take green invisibility potion**
- **Put Feather of Truth** on scale
- **Jump on opposite scale** to heal heart
- **Exit when Lord Azriel appears**

### Completing the Sylph Quest

- **Navigate to narrow passage** where Connor smells mold
- **Collect green mold** from drained blood pool
- **Return to Sylph** and **give rust shield piece** and **green mold**
- **Receive armor-increasing spell**

### The Switch Room

- Navigate to **double doors** (use circular gold key)
- **Kill all skeletons** in 6 boxes plus 2 standing
- Complex switch sequence to open all 16 jail cells:
  1. **Flip down** all 4 east wall switches
  2. **Kill everything** in all 16 cells from distance
  3. **Pull lever** in last north-left cell (opens northern exit)

### The Hammer of Hades

- **Face west doors** and **drink red reveal potion**
- **Kill huge Egyptian Skeleton** through fake door
- **üíæ SAVE** before platform sequence
- **Time jumps** past wandering spirits to center platform
- **Drink sacred water** to heal (spirits drain health)
- **Take hammer** (spirits leave)

### Final Battle

- Navigate to **north central structure** (Commander Skeleton's room)
- **üíæ SAVE** with full health and hammer ready
- **Kill skeleton army** (back away, use crossbow first)
- **Kill Commander Skeleton** with sword (drink shield potion)
- **Take double-edged sword** and other items
- **Throw hammer** at lever across river to lower bridge
- **Cross bridge** and **enter portal** to Swamp

## Swamp

**Character**: Connor | **Location**: Toxic marshlands

### Swamp Dangers

- **Swamp Bubbles**: Deadly underwater creatures - throw hammer when you see/hear them
- **Swamp Slimes**: Light brown four-legged water spiders
- **Swamp Fiends**: Large creatures with big sticks
- ‚ö†Ô∏è **Avoid green toxic water patches**

### The Oracle Tree

- Go **west** to large tree against hill
- **Talk to Oracle** and **give gold** when asked (10 times total)
- Receive several experience boosts

### Swamp Shacks

- Go **southwest** through narrow area to shacks
- **Enter left shack**:
  - **Open trunk** and **take gold**
  - **Take metal gloves** from shelf
- **Enter right shack** (break boards):
  - **Take gold** from chest
  - **Break inner door** and **take white Hearing Horn**
  - **Take more gold** from second chest

### Whispering Weeds

- Go **southeast** from Oracle to area with standing figures
- **Approach carefully** until you hear whispering
- **Use Hearing Horn** on them to learn about Mask piece

### Giant Mushroom Cove

- Go **north** and **northeast** to cove with giant mushrooms
- **Collect small mushrooms**
- **Use sword** to get **piece of Golden Mushroom** from back (Spell ingredient!)

### Mandragor Grove

- Go **northeast** to dangerous tree grove
- **üíæ SAVE** before approaching corpse hanging from root
- **Rush in**, **take crossbow**, and **escape quickly**
- Combat tip: **Cut one root**, then **rush in** and **slash tree**

### Swamp Wisps

- Go **northwest** along hill to sparkling light cove
- **Talk to Swamp Wisps** - they ask for secret
- **Tell them** about secret from Whispering Weeds
- **Take magic flower** and **eat it** (protection from poison water!)
- Learn about **Golden Ladle** needed to cleanse waters

### Swamp Witch's Tower

- Go to **extreme south** of map
- **üíæ SAVE** with high health
- **Use shield and strength potions**
- **Kill Swamp Witch** at south entrance
- **Shoot hanging sandbags** through blocked door to open it

#### Tower Interior

##### First Floor

- **Take rocks** from left room
- **Take Unicorn horn** from dead goblin's chest (right room)
- **Click cauldron** (Henchmen creation movie)
- **Click book** on pedestal
- **Use sword** on holes while climbing ramp (disarms arrow traps)

##### Second Floor

- **Break everything** for useful items
- **Take items** from balcony

##### Third Floor

- **Throw rock** on spike trap at ramp bottom to spring it
- **Take Chain Mail** from east ramp
- **üíæ SAVE** before west ramp
- **Take second Mask piece** from chest
- **Kill appearing Henchman** and **take his hand**

### Secret Vault Access

- Go to **tall scaffolding** in west-central map
- **Climb twice** to reach bridge
- **Use Henchman's hand** on hand print scanner
- Connor moves to stone column puzzle

#### Stone Column Puzzle

```

        O X O O O O O
        X O O X O O O
        O X X O X X O
        O O O O O X O
        O O O O X O O
```

### Completing the Swamp

- **üíæ SAVE** after each successful jump!
- **Enter secret vault** and **kill 2 Swamp Fiends**
- **Take Golden Ladle** from chest
- **Pull 2 chain handles** and **flip switch** (opens gate, disarms grid)
- **Return to Witch's Tower** cauldron
- **Use Golden Ladle** on cauldron to purify swamp
- **Talk to Mudge** (snail king) - creates whirlpool
- **Enter whirlpool** behind tower to reach Underground Realm

## Barren Region

**Character**: Connor | **Location**: Volcanic wasteland

### Fire Ant Assault

- **Immediately back up** to cliff and **ready crossbow**
- **Rapid fire** at fire ants until numbers reduced
- **Go west** picking up mushrooms, **avoid Pyro Demons** in lava river

### Village Area

- **Cross bridge** and **go north** to huts
- **Enter east hut** (weapon shop):
  - **Super battle axe**: 500 gold (buy if affordable)
  - Learn about **Basilisk** and special weapon needed
- **Enter west hut** and **talk to shaman woman**

### The Sanctuary

- **Go behind west hut** and **up path** between pillars
- **Climb into fortress** and **cross footbridge**
- **Talk to creature** with big horn (learns about missing **Stone of Order**)
- **Enter blacksmith shop** and **talk to blacksmith**

### Stone Block Bridge Puzzle

- **Go northwest** along lava to **stone block bridge**
- **Jump onto rising/falling blocks** (up arrow, not INSERT key)
- **Find 7 metal blocks** with shapes (lines, triangles, squares, stars)
- **Count lines** in each shape (line=1, triangle=3, etc.)
- **Jump on metal blocks** in **ascending prime number order**: 1, 3, 5, 7, 11, 13, 17

#### Bridge Navigation Map:

```
O 2   finish  6 O
O O   O O O O   3
5 O   O O 7 O   O
O O   1 O O O   O
O O   O O O 4   O
      start
```

**Sequence**: north, northwest to 1, northwest/north/north to 2, southeast/east to 3, southwest/south to 4, west/northwest to 5, northeast/east to 6, south/southwest to 7

### Fire Gem Collection

- **Watch for fire ants** on north side and **kill them**
- **Avoid lava geysers** and **go northeast** along hill
- **Go north** up incline to **stone pyramid**
- **Take fire gem** from altar (automatically talk to Archon)

### Stone of Order Quest

- **Return south** through geyser field and **go east**
- **Navigate mountain pass** to river with stone bridge sections
- **üíæ SAVE** before crossing - **kill Pyro Demons** in lava with crossbow
- **Cross stone sections** carefully (üíæ SAVE after each jump)
- **DON'T jump final 3 sections east** (army of Pyro Demons there)

### Gong Tower Puzzle

- **Climb building** with grappling hook
- **Get out crossbow** on inscribed platform
- **Shoot 7 gongs** in **rainbow color order** (ROY G BIV):
  - **Red, Orange, Yellow, Green, Blue, Indigo, Violet**
  - **Number gongs 1-7 left to right** (1-3 left, 4 center, 5-7 right)
  - **Shoot order**: 4, 2, 7, 5, 1, 3, 6
- **Cross extended bridge** and **take Stone of Order**

### Basilisk Weapon Creation

- **Return to fortress** and **place Stone of Order** on pedestal
- **Get strong metal shaft** from blacksmith shop
- **Go to weapon shop** and **give metal shaft and black diamond**
- **Pay 10 gold** to receive **Black Diamond Pike**

### Basilisk Battle

- **Go south** across bridge to **far south central** area
- **Go west** up slope behind Basilisk, **bear southeast** through pass
- **Navigate lava stone blocks** to reach cliff behind Basilisk
- **üíæ SAVE** - this jumping sequence is very difficult
- **Climb cliff** and **Connor automatically** jumps on Basilisk with pike
- **Ready ranged weapon** for possible Pyro Demons

## Essential Combat & Strategy Guide

### Combat Effectiveness by Enemy Type

#### Early Game (Daventry)

- **Goblins**: Easy fist/dagger targets for experience
- **Spriggans**: Use crossbow from distance, dangerous up close
- **Henchmen**: Tough enemies requiring shield potions and strong weapons

#### Mid Game (Death/Swamp/Gnomes)

- **Skeletons**: Crossbow from distance, sword for cleanup
- **Rock Demons**: Hand weapons work well, moderate difficulty
- **Swamp Creatures**: Hammer throw for Bubbles, sword for others

#### Late Game (Barren/Frozen/Sun)

- **Pyro Demons**: Crossbow mandatory, lava makes melee impossible
- **Frost Demons**: Slow but lethal, crossbow while backing up
- **Ice Orcs**: Crossbow effective, dangerous in groups

### Equipment Progression

#### Weapons Path

1. **Fists** ‚Üí **Dagger** ‚Üí **Axe** ‚Üí **Sword of Lake**
2. **Crossbow** ‚Üí **Heavy Crossbow** ‚Üí **Ice Crossbow**
3. **War Hammer** ‚Üí **Super Battle Axe** ‚Üí **Black Diamond Pike** ‚Üí **Flame Sword**

#### Armor Path

1. **Leather pieces** ‚Üí **Chain Mail** ‚Üí **Bronze Breastplate**
2. **Full Armor** ‚Üí **Armor of Light** ‚Üí **Permanent Spell effects**

### Resource Management

#### Critical Save Points

- Before difficult jumps (mausoleum roof, ice blocks, lava stones)
- Before major battles (Henchmen, Dragon Wyrm, Basilisk, Drake)
- Before complex puzzles (floating tiles, stone columns, mask assembly)

#### Healing Priority

1. **Healing Springs/Wells** (free, unlimited)
2. **Mushrooms** (abundant, 5 HP each)
3. **Crystals** (50 HP, save for emergencies)
4. **Sacred Water** (100 HP, very rare)
5. **Elixir of Life** (full heal, extremely rare)

### Puzzle Solutions Quick Reference

#### Symbol Sequences

- **Death Floating Tiles**: Strange pitchfork ‚Üí Half circle with dot ‚Üí Circle with dot ‚Üí Back-to-back half circles
- **Sarcophagus/Safe**: Crescent ‚Üí Triangle ‚Üí Circle ‚Üí Square
- **Gong Tower**: 4, 2, 7, 5, 1, 3, 6 (rainbow color order)

#### Pressure Plate Solutions

- **Stone Blocks**: Prime numbers ascending (1, 3, 5, 7, 11, 13, 17)
- **Central Tower**: Use rocks or zombie bodies on 4 corner plates
