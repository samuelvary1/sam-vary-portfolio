## Introduction

King's Quest (2015) is an episodic adventure game that serves as both a prequel and a reboot to the classic King's Quest series. The game follows the adventures of young Graham as he works to become a knight and eventually King of Daventry.

### The Three Paths

Throughout the game, you can choose different approaches that reflect three core values:

- **üß† Wisdom**: Focus on intelligence, problem-solving, and magical solutions
- **‚ù§Ô∏è Compassion**: Emphasize kindness, helping others, and peaceful resolutions
- **‚öîÔ∏è Bravery**: Choose direct action, courage, and bold solutions

These choices affect dialogue, cutscenes, and how NPCs react to Graham, but none are "wrong" choices. The townspeople have preferences:

- **Amaya** the blacksmith prefers bravery
- **Wente** the baker prefers compassion
- **Chester and Muriel** the alchemists prefer wisdom

### General Tips

- **Look everywhere**: Adventure games reward thorough exploration
- **Talk to everyone**: Show items to NPCs and ask all available questions
- **Try everything**: Use items on all interactive objects and people
- **No dead ends**: You cannot get permanently stuck in this game

### Controls

- **Action button**: Context-sensitive for talking, picking up items, shooting bow
- **Movement**: Left stick/arrow keys to move and aim
- **Inventory**: Open to view items and use them on objects/people
- **Note**: You cannot combine items in your inventory

## The Dragon's Well

This opening section serves as both tutorial and backstory, showing Graham's quest for one of the magic treasures before becoming king.

### Getting Through the Well

- Walk up to the well and use the crank
- Climb the ladder down into the well
- Use the action button on the mattress and enter the small door
- Continue down the path to the next screen
- At the two wheels, choose the **right wheel** to continue

üèÜ **Trophy Location - "Napping on the Job"**: On the next screen, hop in the bed several times until Graham admits to taking a nap.

- Head down the long hallway, then across the bridge
- Go all the way right and climb up the rope
- Continue straight past the waterfall and grab the stick with rope to get the **WOODEN HANDLE**
- Hop in the bed on your left to hide
- Head back past the waterfall and walk onto the left ledge
- Use the **WOODEN HANDLE** on the broken wheel
- Cross the bridge, walk along the narrow edge, then go down the ladder

### Navigating the Dragon's Lair

- Walk forward and wait for the spiked boat to move away
- Duck into the left alcove, then when safe, move to the right alcove
- Continue forward when the path is clear
- When you step on crunchy bones, hop in the bed ahead to hide
- Tiptoe carefully around the bones to avoid noise
- Hide in the bed that drops from the ceiling

üèÜ **Trophy Location - "In the Mouth of Danger"**: After hiding, walk straight up to the dragon and let it eat you.

### Getting the Mirror

- Climb the ladder at the end of the left hallway
- Go right and pick up the **BOW** near the skeleton
- Shoot the wheel across the gap, cross the bridge
- Use the wheel nearby twice in a row
- Ring the bell by pulling the rope
- Use the wheel again and quickly climb up the ladder to stand on the meat
- Walk up to the mirror

### Escape Sequence

- Run across the bridge avoiding holes that open up
- Shoot ropes to drop spiked beds as you flow down the river
- Press button prompts to stay on the raft
- When the dragon head appears, shoot the rope above it
- Run through the final section avoiding falling debris
- Return through the small door

### Final Choice

You have three options to save yourself, each representing a different path:

- **Compassion**: Shoot the wheel behind the dragon to free him
- **Bravery**: Shoot the dragon's open eye
- **Wisdom**: Shoot the bell to the right of the dragon

## Arrival in Daventry

### Getting to the Merchant

- Slide down the hill (direction doesn't matter)
- Head down the path to the right after the cutscene
- Take the small path to the left
- Follow the trail to a screen with a bird's nest and toolbox in a tree
- Go down the hill on the right, swing left to reach the tree base
- Shake the tree to get the **HATCHET** from the toolbox
- Continue east, hopping over rocks
- Follow the trail until you meet the merchant

üèÜ **Trophy Location - "Unicorn Snack Food"**: Use the hatchet on the "unicorns" repeatedly until they eat you.

üèÜ **Trophy Location - "A Secret Entrance"**: Go around the "unicorns" and follow the hidden trail behind them to the end.

### Crossing the River

- Use the **HATCHET** on the tree near the river (right side)
- Hop on the log bridge to cross
- Walk down the long, winding, dark trail

üèÜ **Trophy Location - "A Prickly Situation"**: Try to walk through the brambles blocking the path to the well.

### Getting Into Town

- Continue east until you reach the gate
- Climb the rocks on the right side to get over the wall
- The town is empty - you need a "wheel" for the merchant
- Choose from three options (you can only leave one tip):
  - **Blacksmith**: Take the shield, optionally leave a tip
  - **Bakery**: Take the giant loaf of bread, optionally leave a tip
  - **Magic Shop**: Take the tabletop, optionally leave a tip
- Return to the merchant and give them the "wheel"

### The Chivalry Test

After the merchant drops you off:

üèÜ **Trophy Location - "Graham the Basilisk"**: Head east past the beehive tree to the water. Walk through the rightmost reeds to "walk on water," cross to the other side.

- Go east past the beehive tree
- Jump on stepping stones and into the river
- While guards argue, shake the beehive tree
- Return west to the other knight-hopefuls
- Use **HATCHET** on the big tree on the left
- Use the rope on the ground, then try to shoot the tree
- Return east and cross on the rocks and fallen log
- Use **HATCHET** on the log, then use **ROPE** on the logs to make a raft
- Cross to the other side and climb the cliff where Manny went

### Inside the Hall

- Enter and answer the question however you want
- Talk to the man with bullhorns (left side) and tell him your name
- Talk to the taller man on the right side
- Talk to Manny and ask about Acorn
- Return to Acorn and say you know what he's hiding
- Ask his squirrel's name
- Tell the shiny knight in the center that his name is Whisper
- Take the test at the table. Answers:
  1. Manny
  2. Achaka
  3. Sir Walter Harris Ignatius Sally Percival Eduardo Ramon Jr. the Third of Modesto
  4. Princess Madelin of Avalon
- Cross the new bridge and follow the path
- At the fork with the broken wagon, go **west**, then **north** at the pumpkin patch into town

- Grab the **FRUIT PICKER** leaning against the tree
- Go east then south to find a rustling green bush
- Use **STEAK** on the bush, then pick up the **BADGER**

üèÜ **Trophy Location - "Purple Badger Don't Care"**: Return to the potion shop and dip the **BADGER** in the cauldron to turn it purple. Return to the picnic area, shake the tree to drop the beehive, then use the purple **BADGER** on it.

- Return to pumpkin patch and use **BADGER** on the big pumpkin to get **PUMPKIN**

üèÜ **Trophy Location - "Horn Blower"**: Head south of the pumpkin patch to find a big horn. Blow it several times in a row.

**Important for "Helping Hat" Trophy**: To ensure a smooth picnic, use the **FRUIT PICKER** to remove the beehive (don't leave it on the table).

## The Path of Bravery

This is the longest and most complex path. You need a **GOLD COIN** to start.

### Preparing for the Dragon's Well

- Head to Amaya's blacksmith shop in town
- Use a **GOLD COIN** to buy the **GARDENING TOOL** from the wall
- Leave town through the **north** exit (close the door behind you for trophy)
- Follow the upper path to the brambles where you can see the dragon's well
- Use **GARDENING TOOL** to cut through brambles (you'll lose the tool)
- Enter the well

### Inside the Well with Achaka

- Head east along the path
- Use the right wheel to proceed (same as before)
- Follow the trail until you see Achaka slide down the rope

üèÜ **Trophy Location - "Dead End Dancer"**: Instead of following Achaka, continue east as far as possible. Graham will eventually do a little dance at the dead end.

- Use the rope to follow Achaka down

### Jumping Puzzle

Follow Achaka's pattern or use his verbal cues:

- **"Stalama"** means "No"
- **"Affa Nata"** means "Yes"

**Section 1**: Follow Achaka's jumps with verbal guidance
**Section 2**: Same as section 1, but time jumps to avoid the swinging spiked club
**Section 3**: Remember Achaka's pattern (no verbal cues):

- Forward to red barrel
- Forward-right to blueish barrel
- Forward to brown barrel
- Time jump forward-left to big red barrel
- Time jump back-left to blueish barrel
- Time jump forward-left to yellow barrel
- Forward-left to blue barrel
- Forward-right to brown barrel
- Jump to finish

### Action Platforming Section

Navigate the platforming section by:

- Using the action button to jump and grab ledges
- Using the left stick to aim for the next ledge
- Waiting behind cover when areas are open to the background
- Moving quickly through open areas after the dragon breathes fire
- Following the generous checkpoints if you fall

### The Cage Puzzle

When you land in the cage over spikes:

- Yell "Stalama!" when Achaka tries the crank
- Yell "Affa Nata!" when he turns the wheel
- Yell "Affa Nata!" when he returns to the crank
- Go through the small door

üèÜ **Trophy Location - "Opening Knight"**: After the scene, open and close the small door three times.

### Shield and Arrow Puzzle

Complete this sequence carefully:

1. Use the action button on the nearby shield to place it, tell Achaka "Affa Nata"
2. Cross the rope bridge to the other side
3. Push the weapon rack, tell Achaka "Stalama"
4. Place the nearby shield on its stand, tell Achaka "Affa Nata"
5. Use the new rope to cross the large gap south
6. Push the weapon rack to the right, tell Achaka "Stalama"
7. Cross back on the rope
8. Remove the shield directly in front of you, tell Achaka "Affa Nata"
9. Use the right rope bridge to reach the center island by the dragon's tail
10. Put the shield on its stand, say "Affa Nata"
11. Use ropes to return to the landing outside Achaka's cell
12. Pull the rope from the ceiling to ring the bell

### Escaping Together

- Follow Achaka down the path
- Let him boost you into the small opening
- Head south, then west along the path
- Turn the wheel to open the door for Achaka
- Attempt to shoot the arrow at the wooden beam (Achaka will help)
- Follow the path until it narrows, then crawl through the tight tunnel
- When Achaka gets stuck, push his behind to help him through

### Final Choice with the Sleeping Dragon

You have two options:

1. **Tell Achaka "Affa Nata!"** when he aims at the sleeping dragon
   - You'll get the dragon's eye for the competition
2. **Tell Achaka "Stalama!"** and he'll aim at the window instead
   - You won't get the eye (Manny will provide one if needed)

Both choices lead to the same outcome story-wise.

üèÜ **Trophy Location - "That's the Ticket"**: You automatically get this trophy for progressing past the eye competition regardless of which path you chose.

## Goblin Fight

This section is only available after completing Achaka's section in the dragon's well.

### Getting There

- Check the competition board near King's statue to get **WHISPER'S PHOTO** if you haven't
- Go to the wolf cave area (north from King's statue, east through trap, north)
- Head east to the area with rope and tree
- Now that you can shoot properly, use the action button on the rope and aim for the tree across the gap
- Cross the rope and head east along the trail
- Graham will sense being watched - just keep backing out of first-person views
- Continue to the wagon at the end, even when goblins start following

### The Fight

Choose your approach:

**Swift Approach**:

- Aim and shoot to kill all goblins
- Shoot injured goblins again to finish them off
- Show no mercy

**Patient Approach** (Easier):

- Only shoot goblins actively trying to harm you
- Target goblins with spears and the rock-thrower on top
- Let injured goblins walk away

### After the Fight

You can choose to save the merchant or let him be carried away:

**If you save him**: He gives you a **FEATHER** and **GOLD COIN**

**If you don't save him**: Examine the cart and use crosshairs to grab:

- **FEATHER** from the open satchel on the steps
- **GOLD COIN** from inside shelf

### Optional Potion Completion

- If you want the "Helping Hat" trophy, give the **FEATHER** to Chester and Muriel
- They'll give you **INCOMPLETE HYPNOTIC POWDER**
- You'll need a **FROG** later to complete it

## Speed Competition

You need to rescue your steed Triumph before competing. If you already have the **WORK ORDER**, you can skip the retrieval section.

### Getting the Work Order (If Needed)

- Check the competition board at King's statue for **WHISPER'S PHOTO** if you haven't
- You should have the **BLUE FLAME LANTERN** and be friends with Olfie
- Cross Olfie's bridge and go north
- Use the **BLUE FLAME LANTERN** in the dark forest
- Continue to find a big rock with a light beam and **WORK ORDER**
- Take the **WORK ORDER** and head back west

### If You Already Used the Work Order:

If you used it on the boulder for **BITTERROOT**:

- Complete the strength competition first
- Find Acorn stomping near your trapped steed
- Give him his **SOCK** to make friends
- Ask him to help get Triumph down

### Rescuing Triumph:

- Head to the wolf cave and go through to the other side
- Continue to the very end where Triumph is trapped
- Walk up to the tree near Triumph and use **WORK ORDER** on it
- Walk back along the path until Amara arrives with the Crumbler
- Return to Triumph for a scene

### The Race

The speed competition starts automatically. You can't actually lose - if Whisper beats you, Manny will add "one more lap" until you win.

**Lap 1**:

- Move left and right to dodge barriers
- After Whisper takes a nap, jump hay bales and duck under logs using triangle
- Note: Button prompts may disappear quickly, but you still have time to press them

**Lap 2**:

- You'll be in a water wheel - jump hay bales with triangle (different angle)
- You'll lose Triumph and end up on Whisper's back
- Use square and circle to tell Whisper to dodge left or right

**Lap 3**:

- You're on your two new friends, moving slowly
- Use same commands to avoid obstacles and jump hay bales
- Tell Whisper to talk to guards before the hay bale to trip him up
- Tell Whisper to talk to guards again at the bandstand
- Even if you trip him up, he'll still take the lead (fourth lap incoming)

**Lap 4**:

- Jump over obstacles normally
- Tell Whisper to do a handstand under the arch to knock him off
- At the hay bale, tell Whisper to run backwards to trip him
- At the bandstand, tell him to talk to guards and go for the win

### After the Race

- Talk to the crying Whisper and give him his **PICTURE** to make friends
- Ask him to catch a frog for you
- Return to town and give the **FROG** to Chester and Muriel
- Receive the **COLOR-CHANGING POTION** (if you gave them the **FEATHER**, the potion is now complete)

üèÜ **Trophy Location - "Fast Feather"**: You automatically get this trophy for beating Whisper.

## Strength Competition

You can't win with pure strength, so you need to gather some special items first.

### Preparation

- Check the competition board at King's statue for **WHISPER'S PHOTO** if you haven't
- You should have the **BLUE FLAME LANTERN** and be friends with Olfie
- Cross Olfie's bridge east of King's statue
- Go north and use **LANTERN** in the dark woods
- Take the **WORK ORDER** from the big rock with the light beam (if you haven't done speed competition)
- At the end of the dark path, take the **south** path
- Walk east along the ledge during the meeting
- Climb down the rocks on the right side

### Finding the Secret Meeting

- Walk back past the giant door and continue left
- Climb on the ledge and examine the ladder in the hole
- Use the ladder to climb back up and go west
- Pick up the **GOLD COIN** near the cart on the way
- Use Olfie's mouthpiece to call him, but immediately go east instead of crossing
- Use the ladder to go back down and head east for a cutscene

### The Secret Knock Sequence

After hearing Olfie's secret knock:

- Knock on the door
- Knock again
- Shake the bell tree
- Shake the bell tree again
- Knock again
- Say "Truss me"
- Continue conversations as desired
- **Throw the bell down** when prompted (NOT Olfie's mouthpiece)
- Say you're taking your horn and leaving
- **Select Waddles's mouthpiece** (the one on the right)

Important: Leave with both Olfie's and Waddles's mouthpieces. If you mess up, you'll have to repeat this section.

### Dealing with Pillare

- Head left and back up the ladder
- Go west and use Olfie's horn to cross the bridge
- Use Waddles's mouthpiece on his horn (south of town and pumpkin patch)
- Cross over and go west

Choose one option to make Pillare fall asleep:

**Option 1** (For "Helping Hat" trophy):

- Use **HYPNOTIC POTION** on the croissants to the left of Pillare
- Tell her to take a nap after she eats them

**Option 2**:

- Use **FEATHER** on the pile of croissants
- She'll eat it, get drowsy, and fall asleep
- Note: This prevents completing the potion in the magic shop

- Once she's asleep, grab her **MOUTHPIECE**

### Using Pillare's Horn

- Head to the floating island (north from King's statue, east past trap, follow signs)
- Use Olfie's mouthpiece to cross to the center island
- Walk around left and use Pillare's mouthpiece on her horn instead of climbing the ladder

**Optional Castle Visit**: You can now visit the castle up close. Go through the forest north of where you met Olfie, head east, and use Pillare's mouthpiece on her horn there. Follow the path to see the castle exterior (nothing to do there yet).

### The Actual Competition

- Talk to the guard to start
- He'll push you off the left side (tap button anyway) - Pillare will catch you if you used her horn
- Sneak behind Acorn and grab the ball of yarn
- Press X repeatedly until Acorn starts winding up yarn

**Strategy**: Make yarn lines that cross Acorn's path to trip him:

- Create a plus sign or "X" pattern across the center
- Wait for Acorn to approach a yarn line
- Press X to trip him when he reaches the line (timing is key)
- If mistimed, he'll just stumble; keep trying
- When he falls, hit X multiple times to pull more string

**Round 2**: He'll throw you over the edge

- Use Waddles's mouthpiece on his horn while dangling
- Repeat the yarn-crossing strategy with the new post layout

**Round 3**: Thrown over again

- Use Olfie's mouthpiece while dangling
- Make another yarn cross pattern to trip Acorn
- Pull the yarn again for a cutscene

**Final Phase**: Acorn is angry now

- Run to a post and hit X to wave the sock and get his attention
- Move out of the way so he hits the post instead of you
- Repeat for all the posts

### After the Competition

**If you already did speed competition**:

- Head back toward town until you find Acorn
- Give him his **SOCK** to make friends - he gives you a **PAIR OF SOCKS**
- Talk to him again and he'll push the rock so you can get **BITTERROOT**
- Return to town and give the **BITTERROOT** to Wente for **STRUDEL**

**If you used the WORK ORDER on the boulder**:

- Head to where your steed is trapped (through wolf cave)
- Find Acorn stomping around and give him his **SOCK**
- He gives you a **PAIR OF SOCKS**
- Ask him to help get your steed down

üèÜ **Trophy Location - "Strong Minded"**: You automatically get this trophy for beating Acorn.

## Test of Wits

The final competition against Manny requires careful preparation and strategy.

### Getting Inside

- Head to the competition hall - your path is blocked by angry squirrels
- Give them the **STRUDEL** and they'll move aside happily
- Give the guard the **PAIR OF SOCKS** to enter
- Cross the bridge and enter the hall

### The Poisoned Cup Game

- Sit down to start the duel of wits
- No matter which cup you choose first, you'll be hypnotized
- Manny will be fine and you must do what he tells you
- The hypnosis will wear off and you can make moves, but you can't win yet
- The guards will rig it for a rematch (and continue until you win)

**Setting Up Your Advantage**:

- Go up to the cups and use the **COLOR-CHANGING POTION**
- Sit down to play again - now you can tell which drink is which
- You can play without being influenced by the hypnosis powder

### The Strategy Board Game

The goal is to shoot Manny's mechanical man with arrows bounced off shields.

**Game Rules**:

- Each turn you can either rotate a shield/your mechanical Graham OR move them one square
- You cannot move a shield man that Manny moved on his last turn
- After your move, you automatically shoot an arrow

**Easier Victory Method**:
If you lose 3 times total (including the forced first loss), you can say "Let's make things more interesting" when sitting down again. Graham will request two cups instead of three. Since you dyed one cup, you can easily avoid the poison and let Manny get the hypnotic powder. He'll be under your control for the first few moves, letting you set up an easy win.

### The Final Duel

After winning the board game, you have 3 options for the duel:

- Use crosshairs to flick your last **GOLD COIN** to one of the shopkeepers
- Each option represents bravery, wisdom, or compassion
- Complete a few quick-time button events
- You're done!

üèÜ **Trophy Location - "The End?"**: You automatically get this trophy for completing the chapter.

## The Ending

The final screen after the closing cutscene shows the consequences of all your choices throughout the game in pictorial form.

### What Changes Based on Your Choices

1. **Graham's Expression**: Changes based on whether you were more brave, wise, or kind

2. **The Dragon**:

   - No chain if you freed him
   - Eyeless if you shot him
   - Chained if you rang the dinner bell

3. **Amaya the Blacksmith**:

   - Badge and happy face if you kept the town door closed
   - Upset with no badge if wolves got in

4. **Chester and Muriel**:

   - Yellow teeth if you didn't finish their potion
   - White teeth if you completed it

5. **The Eye**: Shows which eye you used for the competition (dragon, jack-o-lantern, or bridge troll)

6. **Olfie's Nickname**: Displays the nickname he gave you (honey bee, goose, or bandicoot)

7. **Wente the Baker**:

   - Covered in bee stings if you ruined his picnic
   - Always holds an engagement ring regardless (love conquers all!)

8. **The Goblins**:

   - Dead goblin under rocks if you killed most of them
   - Goblin with white flag if you let most live

9. **Wagon Wheel**: Shows what you used to replace it (bread, shield, or tabletop)

10. **Final Duel Weapons**: Shows what each shopkeeper is holding based on your choice:
    - Amaya: dagger
    - Chester and Muriel: bombs
    - Wente: pie
