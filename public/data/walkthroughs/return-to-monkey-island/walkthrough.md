## Introduction

Return to Monkey Island marks Ron Gilbert's return to the franchise he created, featuring modern point-and-click adventure design with the classic Monkey Island humor and puzzle-solving. The game offers both casual and hard difficulty modes, with this walkthrough focusing on the casual experience.

### Game Features

- **Modern Design**: Streamlined interface and quality-of-life improvements
- **Classic Humor**: All the wit and charm of the original series
- **Multiple Difficulty Modes**: Casual and Hard modes with different puzzles
- **In-Game Hint System**: Built-in hint book provides tailored assistance
- **Beautiful Art**: Distinctive visual style honoring the series' legacy

## Prologue: An Unexpected Story Begins

### Getting Started

**At the Scurvydogs Shop:**

1. Enter the **Scurvydogs shop** on the right
2. Interact with the **key on a pegleg** hanging on the left
3. Take the **key**
4. Leave the shop

**At the Outhouse:**

1. Go to the **outhouse** on the left
2. Use the **key with pegleg** on the door to unlock it
3. Interact with the little metal thing near the door
4. It's a **slug** - peel it off the floor

**Getting the Scurvydogs:**

1. Return to the **Scurvydogs shop**
2. Give the **slug** to the counter guy
3. He gives you **two Scurvydogs**
4. Leave and head right through the **flower archway**

**Meeting Dee:**

1. In this area, meet **Dee**
2. Explore various activities (optional)
3. Go right until you see a **gate** and open it
4. Continue right and talk to **dad (Guybrush)**

## Part I: A Friendly Place

### Initial Setup

You start on the **Mêlée Island lookout point**.

**Getting the Mission:**

1. Finish talking to the **Lookout** by saying _"I'm mounting my own voyage. Mine."_
2. He directs you to the **Scumm Bar**
3. Go down stairs and right to the **docks**
4. Enter the building and go right
5. Speak to the **Pirate Leaders** near the fireplace
6. Ask about financing an expedition (they refuse)
7. You storm off automatically

**Finding Alternative Employment:**

1. Go to **LeChuck's ship** on the left of the Scumm Bar
2. Talk to the **ghost pirate**
3. Ask about the **swabbie job**
4. You need: a **mop** and a **disguise**
5. Leave docks and go right to **Low Street**

### Making the Mop

**Getting Materials:**

1. **International House of Mouse**:
   - Talk to **Corina** (Voodoo Lady)
   - Buy the **knife** at bottom of screen
   - Buy the **frog** near the knife

**Finding Out About Mop Construction:** 2. **Scumm Bar Kitchen** (far right of bar):

- Talk to the **cook**
- Ask where to buy a mop
- Learn you need to build one from a **mop-handle tree**

**Getting the Map:** 3. **Wally's Map Store** (Low Street, opposite Voodoo Shop):

- Tell Wally you're looking for a tree to make a mop
- He needs a wood sliver to make you a map
- Sneak the **monocle** while talking
- Exit and re-enter to take another **monocle**
- Combine the two **monocles** in inventory

**Getting Wood Sample:** 4. You need to distract the cook to sample his mop 5. **Governor's Mansion** (past Low and High Street):

- Try to borrow **"Ingredients"** book from **Carla**
- She won't let you until you get her to forgive you
- Give the **frog** to **Carla**
- Borrow the **"Ingredients"** book

6. **Scumm Bar Kitchen**:
   - Give **book** to **cook**
   - Take the **mop** while he's distracted
   - Use **knife** on **mop** to get sample
   - Put **mop** back

**Finding the Mop Tree:** 7. Give **wood sample** to **Wally** for the **mop map** 8. Go to **forest** near museum (Mêlée Island overview) 9. Use **mop map** to navigate forest 10. Follow map directions (up, left, right) to reach **mop tree** 11. Use **knife** on tree to get **branch** (mop handle)

**Making the Mop Head:**
Choose one of these four options:

- **Tangled string** (Scumm Bar kitchen)
- **Shrunken head** (Voodoo shop)
- **Sea sponge** (Stan's old shipyard)
- **Stuffed animal** (Governor's mansion)

Combine **handle** with chosen item to make complete **mop**.

### Getting a Disguise

**Getting the Serial Number:**

1. **Forest Museum**: Follow signage to museum
2. Use **double monocle** on display case lock
3. Note the **serial number**

**Getting the Key:** 4. **Locke Smith Shop** (High Street):

- Give her the **serial number**
- Pick up **crackers** while there

**Getting the Eye Patch:** 5. **Museum**:

- Use **crackers** on **guard parrot**
- Use **key** on display case to take **eye patch**

**Charging the Eye Patch:** 6. **Voodoo Lady** (Low Street):

- Give **eye patch** to **Corina**
- She charges it with magic

**Getting the Job:** 7. **LeChuck's Ship** (docks):

- Put on **enchanted eye patch** before approaching
- Give **mop** to the **quartermaster**

## Part II: A Dangerous Journey

### Initial Ship Exploration

**In the Hold:**

1. Pick up **pamphlet** near ladder
2. Open **crate** to find **Murray** hiding inside
3. Use **mop** to swab **grease** onto porthole (repeat 3 times)
4. Escape through greased **porthole**

**Getting Murray:**

1. Outside, go right and climb over **railing**
2. **LeChuck** on deck needs a flattened skull
3. Return to hold and take **Murray** (convince or force him)

**Preparing Murray:**

1. Go to **top deck**, then down stairs into ship
2. Use **Murray** with **laundry machine** to flatten him
3. Return to **top deck** and put flattened **Murray** in **cauldron**

**Spotting Madison's Crew:**

1. Climb **mast** at back of ship to **crow's nest**
2. Use **spyglass** to look around
3. Go all the way right until you spot **Madison's crew**
4. Talk to **Iron Rose** on top deck about your plan
5. You must convince each crew member to vote for Monkey Island

### Getting the Crew Votes

**Flair's Vote:**

1. Talk to **Flair** (lower deck near kitchen)
2. Her **knife** is stuck in **Gullet's** back
3. From greased porthole, go left and climb into ocean
4. Go down to **rudder** and pull **knife** from **Gullet's** back
5. Return **knife** to **Flair**

**Flambe's Vote:**

1. Talk to **Flambe** in **crow's nest**
2. He wants **Scorched Alaska** (his favorite food)
3. Talk to **Putra** (left side of lower deck) about making one
4. She wants a **promotion** first
5. Get her **promotion request papers**

**Getting Putra's Promotion:**

1. Go to **LeChuck's cabin** (may need to enter twice)
2. Complete **First Swab's Report** before getting promotion
3. Examine report for instructions
4. Log 5 untidy areas using report:
   - **Dirty dishes** in galley
   - **Greasy porthole** in hold
   - **Gullet** beneath ship
   - **Potion spill** on top deck
   - **Empty boxes** on front of top deck
5. Get **Iron Rose** to initial completed report
6. Combine **Putra's promotion request** with **stack of papers**
7. Give **stack** to **LeChuck**
8. Give **signed promotion request** to **Putra**
9. Ask her to make **Scorched Alaska**
10. Give **Scorched Alaska** to **Flambe**

**Apple Bob's Vote:**

1. Ask **Flambe** about his **book** and borrow it
2. Give **book** to **Apple Bob** on top deck

**Iron Rose's Vote:**

1. Talk to **Iron Rose** on top deck
2. Ask about her loyalty to LeChuck
3. Show her the **inspirational onboarding pamphlet**

**Apple Putra's Vote:**
Once you have **Iron Rose's** vote, just ask **Putra** about her vote.

**Calling the Vote:**
Ring the **bell** on top deck **once** to vote.

### Getting the Map

**Stealing LeChuck's Map:**

1. Ring **bell** on top deck **twice** to summon **LeChuck**
2. When he starts shouting, sneak to his **cabin**
3. Swap **LeChuck's map** with **mop map** in inventory

**Getting to Monkey Island:**

1. Pick up **Murray** on top deck (if not already done)
2. Put **Murray** in **cannon** on top deck
3. Fire **cannon** to send him to **Madison's ship**

## Part III: Return to Monkey Island

### Finding the Secret

**Following the Map:**

1. Follow signage pointing right to reach **beach**
2. Go into **jungle** behind beach
3. Use **LeChuck's map** to find The Secret:

**Map Directions:**

- Begin at **red berry bush** (north of beach)
- Follow **stone monkey hand** (take path right)
- Go by **wooden chicken** (take path north)
- Sneak past **bees** and heed sign (take path right)
- Pick up **shovel** and dig at **big X**

**Meeting Madison's Crew:**

1. **Madison's crew** shows up after digging
2. After talking, trek to **volcano beach** (west coast)
3. Go into **ocean** on left and walk until you find **anchor**

### LeChuck's Personal Information

**Getting Gullet's Notebook:**

1. Climb **anchor chain** and go left to find **Gullet**
2. Ask about his **notebook** repeatedly
3. Offer to help with his poetry
4. Ask to see his poetry - he gives you **notebook**

**Boarding LeShip:**

1. Try to go to **top deck** - **Flair** kicks you out
2. Find your partners at **giant monkey head** (east end)
3. Talk about trouble boarding ship
4. Return to ship and recite **disguise poem**
5. You can now board the ship

**LeChuck's Catch Phrase:**

1. Go to **lower deck** and sneak into **LeChuck's cabin**
2. Take **LeChuck's diary** from shelf and read it
3. **February 17 entry** contains his current catchphrase

**LeChuck's Favourite Food:**

1. Pick **daisies** growing near fence by **giant monkey head**
2. Talk to **Putra** in ship's **galley** about LeChuck's eating
3. She says his favorite is **tentacles with daisy sauce**
4. Give her **daisies** and pick up the **dish**

**LeChuck's Theme Song:**
Take **Flair's sheet music** from **lower deck** (left near kitchen)

### The Giant Monkey Head

**Collecting Skulls:**
Pick up six skulls (besides Murray):

- **Southern beach** (where you first arrived)
- **Shipwreck** (eastern side of island)
- **Volcano Beach** (western side)
- **Ocean under LeChuck's ship**
- **Near fence** at giant monkey head
- **Clifftop** near centre of island

**The Musical Puzzle:**

1. Head to **giant monkey head** (east end of island)
2. Go right until you find **Murray**
3. Grab **arm bone** near stakes
4. Place all **skulls** on **stakes**
5. Use **arm bone** on each skull to learn their notes
6. Play notes **"Do Do Mi Sol Fa Fa Re"** by bonking correct skulls
7. When asked, use **catchphrase** from LeChuck's diary (February 17)
8. Eat the **tentacles**

**Leaving Monkey Island:**

1. After falling from cliff, pick up **ship repair manual** Elaine left
2. Optionally collect **Murray** and other **skulls**
3. Meet **Elaine** and use **ship repair manual** on **shipwreck** (east side)

## Part IV: Things Get Complicated

### Initial Confrontation

**At the Voodoo Shop:**

1. Confront **Voodoo Lady** about The Secret
2. After talking with her and **Widey Bones**
3. Go to **Wally's Maps-n-More**
4. Ask **Wally** for **sea map** of local area

### Getting the Golden Keys

**Brrr Muda Golden Key:**

_Scurvy Island Preparation:_

1. Sail to **Scurvy Island**
2. Head north to **camp**
3. Take **firewood** next to tent
4. Take **painting** on right table
5. Read **Elaine's note** and follow directions
6. Find **Elaine** in **lime grove** at landmark intersection
7. Pick up **limes** after talking

_Brrr Muda Customs:_

1. Sail to **Brrr Muda Island**
2. Fill out customs form with **limes**
3. **Don't check** first box (pirate question)
4. Don't worry about other boxes except last one

_Ice Castle Contests:_

1. Go left to island centre, then enter **Ice Castle** (east)
2. Pick up **box of matches** near fire
3. Go upstairs and talk to **Ice Queen**
4. Challenge her to contests:

**Contest of Heartiness:**

- Check **Fish of the Day** sign in contest room
- Buy required fish from **Mêlée fish shop**
- Get **Demon Pepper** from **LeChuck's ship galley**
- Place purchased fish in bucket with **Demon Pepper**
- Ring triangle, then put different fish on your plate

**Contest of Intelligence:**

- Place **firewood** in fireplace (Contest room)
- Light with **matches** from throne room entrance
- Start contest with triangle
- Swap papers with queen while she looks at dripping icicle

**Contest of Seriousness:**

- Get **cookbook** back from **Scumm Bar cook**
- Return to **Carla** at **Governor's mansion**
- Borrow **"The Endless Tale..."** (top left bookshelf)
- Give to **Apple Bob** on **LeChuck's ship**
- Challenge **Ice Queen** to contest
- Use **joke book** to tell queen a joke

**Terror Island Golden Key:**

1. Sail to **Terror Island**
2. Pick up **silver key** from skeleton's ribcage (unlucky place)
3. Get **forgotten lamp** (far right of mysterious clearing)
4. Light **lamp** with **matches**
5. Go to **twisting path** and follow to end
6. Use **knife** on **strange sign**
7. Use **silver key** to open **gate**
8. Enter **dark cave**
9. Go through **twisty passages** without markings
10. Keep exploring until you **fall**
11. **Herman Toothrot** has the key near his bed
12. Tell Herman you want to make a **deal**
13. Demand the **key**
14. Read **XYZZY sign** in inventory to escape

**Old Mrs Smith's Golden Key:**

1. Go to **Mêlée Island**
2. Talk to **Locke Smith** (High Street shop)
3. Ask about her name and relationship to **Old Mrs. Smith**
4. Ask about **Golden Keys**
5. Ask if she'll sell the key
6. She gives it to you **free**

**Widey's Golden Key:**

1. Find **Widey** on **High Street** (Mêlée Island)
2. Ask for her key (you need other keys first to impress her)
3. Based on her description, **Iron Rose** has stolen it
4. Sail to **LeChuck's ship**
5. Pick up **Widey's key** at **Iron Rose's desk** (near LeChuck's cabin)

**Bella Fisher's Golden Key:**

_Getting Stan Free:_

1. Go to **shipyard** on **Mêlée**, take **picture** from office
2. Find **nesting bird** on **Scurvy Island** in **lime grove**
3. Get your **picture** from bird's nest on ground
4. Examine **picture frame**, place your picture next to **Stan's**
5. Show **guard** at **ice quarry** the frame with both pictures
6. Enter **ice quarry** on **Brrr Muda** and find **Stan** (top right corner)
7. Ask if he needs help busting out (he refuses - only 1 month left)

_Extending Stan's Sentence:_

1. Find **Stan's accountant Ned** on **Barebones island** (below 19th parallel)
2. Show **Ned** the **judicial order** for Stan's imprisonment
3. Pick up **Ned's financial records**
4. Find **judge** in **courthouse** on **Brrr Muda**
5. Show him **Stan's financial records**, claim you're **Ned Filigree**
6. Return to **Stan** in **ice quarry**, show **updated papers**
7. Use **double monocle** on **Stan's shackles** for serial number
8. Go to **locksmith** on **Mêlée**, give her **serial number**
9. Return and unlock **Stan's shackles** with new key

_Getting Bella's Flag:_

1. **Stan** creates new **pamphlet** - pick it up
2. Show **scurvy flyer** to **quarantined pirates** on adrift ship
3. Take **Bella Fisher's flag**
4. Combine **flag** with **sea map** to reveal secret fishing spot
5. Go to spot and **dive into ocean**
6. Search **shipwreck** for **Bella Fisher's remains**
7. Take **Golden Key** from bow of wrecked ship

**Opening the Safe:**
Return to **Voodoo Shop** on **Mêlée** and use each **Golden Key** on safe inside.

## Part V: Beneath the Monkey Head

### The Stone Monkey Puzzles

**Getting Inside:**

1. Talk to **Elaine** until you arrive at **Giant Monkey Head**
2. Walk to ledge on far right
3. Jump to **Certain Lava Death**
4. Enter **stairs leading down**

**First Level - "Only the organized pirate will pass":**

1. Read **plaque** on door blocking stairway
2. Explore chambers and collect:
   - **Stone knife**
   - **Stone crackers**
   - **Stone spyglass**
   - **Stone mug**
3. Give appropriate objects to monkey statues:
   - **Stone spyglass** → **squinting monkey**
   - **Stone crackers** → **monkey with parrot**
   - **Stone knife** → **frustrated monkey**
4. Pick up **pieces of eight** (stone pebbles) from chamber floors
5. Door opens - proceed to lower level

**Second Level - "Only the unfair pirate will pass":**

1. Distribute **stone pieces of eight** based on treasure chest sizes:
   - **Captain Monkeybeard** → **3 pieces of eight**
   - **First Swab** → **2 pieces of eight**
   - **Cabin monkey** → **1 piece of eight**
2. Door opens - go down another level

**Third Level - Broken statue:**

1. Explore chambers and use **knife** on:
   - **Sad monkey**
   - **Screaming monkey**
2. Final statue is destroyed - need to fill drain below it
3. Fill **mug** with **oily liquid** from stabbed statues
4. Pour **oily liquid** into **drain** beneath broken statue
5. Final door opens - go down stairs

### The Final Code Wheel

**The Stone Slab Puzzle:**

1. Peer at **stone slab** sinking into lava below **code wheel**
2. Read limerick describing pirate's face (top and bottom halves)
3. Turn **outer and inner wheels** until halves match description
4. Change **date** in middle to match year limerick was published
5. Door opens beneath **code wheel** - enter it

**The Amusement Park:**

1. Explore the **amusement park** area
2. When ready, turn off all lights
3. Use **Stan's key ring** on **light switches**

**Final Completion:**
The game concludes as you experience the ultimate revelation of The Secret of Monkey Island.
