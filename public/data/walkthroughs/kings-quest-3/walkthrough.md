# King's Quest III: To Heir Is Human - Walkthrough

**Maximum total points:** 210

⏰ **CRITICAL:** Keep an eye on the game timer! Everything needs to be done within certain time limits as described below, otherwise Gwydion will die. Save frequently!

## LLEWDOR - The Wizard's Domain

### Initial Tasks (First 5 Minutes)

#### Manannan's House - Starting Tasks

⚠️ **Within the first 5 minutes**, complete the task Manannan gave you (sweep the kitchen floor, feed the chickens, etc.)

#### Kitchen - Gathering Supplies (6 points)

- **Take bread** (1), **take fruit** (1) and **take mutton** (1) from the table (3 points)
- **Take knife** (1) and **take spoon** (1) from the rack on the back wall (2 points)
- **Take the bowl** from the left shelf (1 point)

#### Dining Room (1 point)

- **Take cup** from the table (1 point)

⏰ **After 5 minutes** the wizard shows up in the dining room and announces he will leave for a while. He will return home after half an hour, so be fast and keep an eye on the clock!

---

### Exploring the House (While Manannan is Away)

#### Cat (1 point)

- **Take cat** and **take hair** (1 point)

#### Wizard Bedroom (12 points)

- **Open nightstand drawer** and you'll take the hand mirror (1 point)
- **Open cupboard**, **move clothes** and you'll get a map (7 points)
- **Close cupboard**. **Look top of cupboard** to find and take a small brass key (3 points)
- **Open drawer** of the closet on the right to get Rose Petal Essence (1 point)

#### Attic (1 point)

- **Look floor** and **take fly** (1 point)

#### Chickens (1 point)

- **Open gate**, **take chicken**, **take feather** (1 point), **open gate**

#### Study - Secret Access (10 points)

- **Open door** (of cabinet on the left) to find and **take the magic wand** (4 points)
- **Move book** at the right bookshelves and **turn lever** to open a trap door (5 points)

#### Basement - First Spell and Ingredients (16 points)

- **Look shelf** at the back. **Take toad spittle** (1), **take saffron** (1), **take toad stool powder** (1), **take mandrake root powder** (1), **take nightshade juice** (1), **take fishbone powder** (1) (6 points)

**SAVE** the game, **look table**, **open page IV**, prepare the **"Flying Like an Eagle or a Fly" spell**:

- Put a pinch of saffron in essence
- Recite the following verse:
- \*"Oh winged spirits set me free
- Of earthly bindings, just like thee.
- In this essence, behold the might
- To grant the precious gift of flight."\*
- Wave the magic wand (10 points)

#### Study - Exit Preparation

• **Turn lever**, **move book**, **open door** (cupboard on the left)
• Exit the house and go down the path to the village (**SAVE OFTEN**). Then walk 2 screens south.

---

### Village Exploration

#### Bear House (3 points)

When bears are not present, **open door**, **take porridge** from the table (2 points)
_If the porridge is not there, go outside and exit/reenter the screen until the family arrives home. Then exit/reenter until they leave the house again. Enter the house again and the porridge should be there._
• Go upstairs, **open drawer**, and **take thimble** (1 point)

#### Bear Garden (1 point)

• **Look flowers**, **take dew** (1 point)
• Go 1 screen east to the tree at the coast

#### Coast (1 point)

• **Take mistletoe** (1 point)
• Return to Manannan's house (**SAVE OFTEN**)

---

### Second Wizard Encounter (Around 30-35 Minutes)

#### Gwydion's Room (4 points)

• **Hide all** (4 points) and **take fruit**

#### Kitchen

⏰ The wizard appears when the clock hits around 30 minutes.

#### Dining Room

• **Give fruit to wiz**. He will then disappear and around 35 minutes reappear to say he will go to sleep.
⏰ **You now have until around 60:00 to do all of the following.**

---

### Major Quest Sequence (35-60 Minutes)

#### Gwydion's Room - Teleportation

• **Take all**, **look map**, **F6**, and teleport to the foot of the mountain

#### Village Tavern (3 points)

• Enter the tavern, **look men**, **put fly in essence** and **fly towards men** to hear conversation (3 points)
• Fly out, **"fly begone myself return"**, and go 2 screens west

#### Oak Tree - Robber's Den (14 points)

• **Look hole**, **put fly in essence** and **fly into the tree hole** (5 points)
• Fly out again, **"fly begone myself return"**
• **Reach hole** to reveal a ladder (3 points)
• **Climb ladder** (2), **look into door** (move down and up the ladder again if Gwydion sees movement, until he sees somebody sleep). Then **enter** and **take purse** (4 points) (6 points)
• Exit the house, climb down and go to the foot of the mountain

#### Mountain Foot (2 points)

• An eagle flies by and loses a feather. **Get feather** (2 points)
• Walk one east and one north

#### Cave - Oracle Visit (7 points)

• **Look cave**, **put eagle feather in essence**, and **fly into the web** (4 points)
• Enter the cave. The oracle will reveal your true identity and talk about the three-headed dragon in Daventry. She also gives a stone of amber (3 points)

#### Village Store (5 points)

• Enter the store, **look shelves**. **Buy lard** (1), **buy salt** (1), **buy pouches** (1), **buy fish oil** (1) (4 points)
• **Look dog** and **pet dog** (collecting some dog hair) (1 point)
• Return to Manannan's house

---

### Advanced Spell Preparation

#### Study Access

• As before, **open door** of the left cabinet, **move book**, **turn lever**

#### Basement - Cat Transformation Spell (10 points)

**Open page XXV**, prepare cookie for **"Transforming Another into a Cat" spell**:
• Put the mandrake root powder in a bowl
• Put the cat hair in the bowl
• Put two spoons of fish oil in the bowl
• Stir the mixture with a spoon
• Put the dough on the table
• Pat the dough into a cookie
• Recite the following verse:

- \*"Mandrake root and hair of cat
- Mix oil of fish and give a pat
- A feline from the one who eats
- This appetizing magic treat."\*
  • Wave the magic wand (10 points)

#### Study

• **Turn lever**, **move book**, **open door**

#### Gwydion's Room - Final Preparation

• **Hide all**, **take porridge**, **take cat cookie**, and **put cookie into porridge**

#### Kitchen - The Transformation (12 points)

⏰ Wait for Manannan (timer around 1 hour)

#### Dining Room - Victory Over Manannan (12 points)

• **Give porridge to wiz**. Manannan will change into a cat! (12 points)

---

### Freedom and Advanced Spells

#### Gwydion's Room - Teleportation

• **Take all**, **look map**, **F6**, and teleport to the coast, one screen east of the cave

#### Ingredient Gathering (4 points)

• **Coast**: **Take water** (1 point)
• **Cave**: **Take mud** near the river (1 point)
• Go west until you see the beginning of the desert. **SAVE** the game.

#### Desert - Medusa Encounter (3 points)

• Prepare the words **"use mirror"**, go in the desert, turn your back to the west, and when Medusa shows up, execute the command (**"use mirror"**). Medusa will turn into stone (5 points)
• In the desert, go one screen north and **take cactus** (1 point)
• Go 2 screens north and **take snake** (snake skin) (1 point)

#### Oak Tree (1 point)

• **Take acorns** (on different locations until you find 3 dried acorns) (1 point)
• Return to Manannan's house

---

### Final Spell Preparation (40 points)

#### Study Access

• **Open door**, **move book**, **turn lever**

#### Basement - Multiple Advanced Spells

**Language of Creatures Spell (10 points)**
**Open page II**, prepare spell for **"Language of Creatures"**:
• Put the small feather in a bowl
• Put the fur in the bowl  
• Put the reptile skin in the bowl
• Add a spoonful of powdered fish bone
• Put a thimbleful of dew in the bowl
• Mix with hands
• Separate mixture into two pieces
• Put dough pieces into your ears
• Recite the following verse:

- \*"Feather of fowl and bone of fish,
- Molded together in this dish,
- Give me wisdom to understand
- Creatures of air, sea and land"\*
  • Wave the magic wand (10 points)

**Teleportation Spell (10 points)**
**Open page VII**, prepare spell for **"Teleportation at Random"**:
• Grind a spoon of salt in a mortar
• Grind the mistletoe in the mortar
• Rub the stone in the mixture
• Kiss the stone
• Recite the following verse:

- \*"With this kiss I thee impart,
- Power most dear to my heart.
- Take me now from this place hither,
- To another place far thither."\*
  • Wave the magic wand (10 points)

**Sleep Spell (10 points)**
**Open page XIV**, prepare spell for **"Causing a Deep Sleep"**:
• Grind the acorns in a mortar
• Put the acorn powder in a bowl
• Put the nightshade juice in the bowl
• Stir the mixture with a spoon
• Light a charcoal brazier
• Heat the mixture on the brazier
• Spread the mixture on a table
• Recite the following verse:

- \*"Acorn powder ground so fine
- Nightshade juice, like bitter wine,
- Silently in darkness you creep
- To bring a soporific sleep"\*
  • Wave the magic wand
  • Put the sleep powder in the pouch (10 points)

**Invisibility Spell (10 points)**
**Open page CLXIX**, prepare spell for **"Becoming Invisible"**:
• Cut the cactus with a knife
• Squeeze the cactus juice onto a spoon _(note: corrected from manual)_
• Put the cactus juice in a bowl
• Put the lard in the bowl
• Add two drops of toad spittle
• Stir the mixture with a spoon
• Recite the following verse:

- \*"Cactus plant and horny toad
- I now start down a dangerous road
- Combine with fire and mist to make
- Me disappear without a trace."\*
  • Wave the magic wand
  • Put ointment in the empty lard jar (10 points)

**Storm Spell (10 points)**
**Open page LXXXIV**, prepare spell for **"Brewing a Storm"**:
• Put a cup of ocean water in a bowl
• Light a charcoal brazier
• Heat the bowl on the brazier
• Put a spoon of mud in the bowl
• Add a pinch of toadstool powder
• Blow into the hot brew
• Recite the following verse:

- \*"Elements from the earth and sea,
- Combine to set the heavens free.
- When I stir this magic brew,
- Great god Thor, I call on you."\*
  • Wave the magic wand
  • Pour the storm brew into the jar (10 points)

#### Final Departure (5 points)

• **Look map**, **F6**, and teleport to the village

#### Tavern - Passage Booking (3 points)

• **Look men**, **talk to man**, and **give coins** (3 points)

#### Dock - Departure (2 points)

• **Enter the ship** (2 points)

## SHIP - The Sea Journey

### Below Deck - Preparation (2 points)

• Go right, **take box**, go left, **put box on floor** (left of other crate)
• **Jump box**, **jump box**, **jump ladder** and **climb up** (2 points). **SAVE** the game.

### Middle Deck - Recovery (4 points)

• Go into captain's cabin (if not present), **open chest**, **look into chest** to recover personal possessions, **close chest** (3 points)
• Go right and **take shovel** (1 point)

### Below Deck - Escape Preparation

• Listen to the mice conversations (including about the location of a treasure) and wait until a sailor calls **"land ho"**
• **Pour sleep powder** on the ground (right side), go left, **jump box**, **jump box**, **jump ladder**, and climb to the upper deck

### Upper Deck - Escape (5 points)

• **Jump in water** and swim right to the island (stay close to the edge of the screen to dodge shark attacks) (5 points)

---

## WAY TO DAVENTRY - The Final Journey

### Palm Tree Island - Treasure Hunt (7 points)

• **Dig** on the height of the palm tree, just before the rock on the right of it to uncover a chest (7 points)
• Go north and follow the mountain path, climbing the large rock on the way

### Mountain Path - Yeti Encounter (4 points)

• Climb the small waterfall you encounter on the way. While climbing, prepare the command **"rub stone"**. When the yeti appears, execute the command to teleport. Take the path forking to the left bottom of the screen (4 points)

### Caverns

• Find way through the mountain with the caverns

### Mountain Path - Identity Revelation

• Keep following the path until you fall near a cave entrance
• Go one West and one north and **talk to the old man** to learn your real name
• Return to the cave entrance and go up the long stairs

### Land in Clouds - Final Battle (14 points)

• **Rub ointment on body**. Go one screen west and **"stir storm brew with finger"**. Near the dragon, **"brew of storms, churn it up"** to kill the dragon (7 points)
• **Untie girl** (3 points)
• **Talk to girl** (multiple times). Go down the stairs, west, and twice north to arrive at castle Daventry (4 points)

**THE END - 210/210 points**

## Critical Tips and Time Management

### Time Management Strategy

• **0-5 minutes**: Complete initial chore, gather kitchen items
• **5-30 minutes**: Explore house while Manannan is away, prepare fly spell
• **30-35 minutes**: Brief Manannan encounter
• **35-60 minutes**: Major quest sequence (tavern, robber's den, oracle, store)
• **60+ minutes**: Transform Manannan, complete remaining spells

### Essential Survival Tips

• **Save frequently** - This game is unforgiving with timing
• **Watch the clock** constantly - Missing deadlines means death
• **Prepare commands** in advance for time-critical moments (like Medusa)
• **Gather all ingredients** systematically before attempting spells
• **Follow spell recipes exactly** - incorrect ingredients cause failure

### Spell Recipe Summary

1. **Flying Spell**: Saffron + essence + incantation
2. **Cat Transformation**: Mandrake root + cat hair + fish oil + incantation
3. **Language of Creatures**: Feather + fur + snake skin + fish bone + dew
4. **Teleportation**: Salt + mistletoe + amber stone + incantation
5. **Sleep Spell**: Acorns + nightshade juice + heating
6. **Invisibility**: Cactus juice + lard + toad spittle + incantation
7. **Storm Spell**: Ocean water + mud + toadstool powder + heating

### Navigation Notes

• **Desert**: Turn back to west before using mirror on Medusa
• **Ship**: Stay near screen edges when swimming to avoid sharks  
• **Mountain**: Use teleportation stone when yeti appears
• **Dragon**: Use storm spell for the final battle

This is one of the most challenging King's Quest games due to its strict timing requirements and complex spell system. Patience and careful planning are essential for success!
